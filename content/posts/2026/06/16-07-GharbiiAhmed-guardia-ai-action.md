## What's Changed

First release of the Guardia AI EU AI Act Compliance Scanner.

  Automatically scans your repository for 28+ AI libraries and posts a compliance
  report on every pull request — no account required.

  ## What's included
  - Detects OpenAI, LangChain, TensorFlow, HuggingFace, facial recognition, and 23+ more
  - Classifies risk level: Prohibited / High Risk / Limited / Minimal
  - Posts a detailed PR comment with EU AI Act article references
  - Optional: connect your Guardia AI account for full Article 9–14 analysis
  - Fails CI on prohibited AI practices (configurable)

  ## Enforcement deadline
  EU AI Act is enforceable from August 2, 2026. Start free at https://guardia-ai.com/free-audit
