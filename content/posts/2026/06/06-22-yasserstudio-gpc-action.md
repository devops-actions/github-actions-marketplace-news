## What's Changed

Publish to Google Play from GitHub Actions, with a built-in preflight compliance gate.

- Upload an .aab/.apk to any track in one step.
- Preflight runs Play policy scanners before upload and fails the job on findings.
- Drop-in migration from r0adkll/upload-google-play: keep your existing inputs.
- An `args` escape hatch runs any gpc command.

See the README for usage and the migration table.
