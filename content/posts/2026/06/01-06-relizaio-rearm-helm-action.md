## Action Summary

The `rearm-helm-action` is a GitHub Action designed to automate the versioning, packaging, and publishing of Helm charts to OCI-compliant registries, AWS ECR, or ChartMuseum, while also integrating with ReARM to submit release metadata. It simplifies the process of maintaining and releasing Helm charts by updating the chart version, committing changes, and handling artifact publication and metadata tracking. Additional capabilities include support for SBOM generation, signature verification, and integration with SecureSBOM for enhanced security.
