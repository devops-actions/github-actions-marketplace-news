## Action Summary

The **GreenIT Analysis GitHub Action** automates the evaluation of your web application's environmental impact by running the GreenIT-Analysis CLI against specified URLs. It generates an EcoIndex score report, enforces a quality gate to ensure scores meet a configurable threshold, and uploads the report as an artifact in the CI pipeline. This action streamlines the integration of sustainable development practices into your CI process by identifying and addressing environmentally inefficient web pages.
