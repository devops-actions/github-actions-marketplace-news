## Action Summary

NeuroLink is an enterprise AI integration platform designed to unify access to 13 major AI providers and 100+ models through a consistent API. It streamlines the development and operation of AI-powered applications by enabling provider switching, cost optimization, failover support, and advanced features like Redis persistence and multi-provider routing. Delivered as a TypeScript SDK and CLI, it automates AI integration tasks, enhances scalability, and simplifies the deployment of production-ready AI systems.
