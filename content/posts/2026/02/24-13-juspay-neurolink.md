## Action Summary

NeuroLink is an enterprise-grade AI development platform that unifies access to 13 major AI providers and over 100 models through a single, consistent API. It streamlines AI integration into applications by enabling provider switching, cost optimization, multi-provider failover, and advanced tools like Redis persistence and intelligent routing. Offered as a TypeScript SDK and CLI, it automates and simplifies the development, deployment, and scaling of AI-powered features for production environments.
