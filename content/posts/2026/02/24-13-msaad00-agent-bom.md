## Action Summary

Agent-bom is a GitHub Action designed to generate an AI Bill of Materials (AI-BOM) and perform advanced security analysis for AI agents and MCP servers. It automates tasks such as CVE scanning, blast radius mapping, credential exposure detection, and privilege analysis, providing insights into the impact of vulnerabilities and potential attack surfaces. The tool enhances enterprise security by integrating multiple compliance frameworks (OWASP, MITRE, NIST) and enabling policy enforcement in CI/CD pipelines.
