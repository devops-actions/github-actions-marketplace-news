## Action Summary

This GitHub Action automates the generation of a podcast RSS feed (`podcast.xml`) from a YAML configuration file (`feed.yaml`). It simplifies the process of creating and maintaining podcast feeds that are compatible with major directories like Apple Podcasts and Spotify, ensuring your feed stays updated with new episodes. The action streamlines feed generation by converting episode metadata into a valid RSS format and committing the output back to the repository.
