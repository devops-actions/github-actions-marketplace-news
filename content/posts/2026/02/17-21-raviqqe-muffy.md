## Action Summary

Muffy is a GitHub Action designed to validate static websites by detecting issues such as broken links or other structural problems. It automates the process of ensuring a website's integrity and functionality, saving time and reducing the risk of errors in manual validation. This tool is particularly useful for developers maintaining static sites who need a reliable way to identify and address potential issues early in the development workflow.
