## Action Summary

The **Pixi-Pack GitHub Action** automates the creation of cross-platform, self-extracting binaries for Pixi environments, enabling seamless packaging and distribution for macOS, Linux, and Windows. It simplifies the process of packaging environment dependencies into a portable format, ensuring consistency and reproducibility across platforms. This action is ideal for automating the build and release workflows of projects utilizing Pixi environments.
