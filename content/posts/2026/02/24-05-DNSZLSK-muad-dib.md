## Action Summary

MUAD'DIB is a supply chain security tool designed to detect and analyze threats in npm and PyPI dependencies. It combines static and dynamic analysis, deobfuscation, anomaly detection, and ground truth validation to identify malicious packages and suspicious patterns, providing actionable insights and risk scores. This action automates pre-installation scans and streamlines threat response, offering developers a quick, free first line of defense against dependency-related attacks.
