## Action Summary

Agent-bom is an AI-focused vulnerability analysis and risk mapping tool that generates a detailed Bill of Materials for AI agents and MCP servers. It automates CVE detection, blast radius mapping, privilege analysis, and credential exposure assessments while integrating security frameworks like OWASP LLM Top 10, MITRE ATLAS, and NIST AI RMF. This action enables organizations to identify compromised AI agents, assess business impact, and enforce security policies efficiently in CI/CD pipelines.
