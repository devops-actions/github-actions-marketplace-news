## Action Summary

The `tt-gds-action` GitHub Action automates the process of hardening a Tiny Tapeout design into a manufacturable GDS (Graphic Data System) file. It simplifies the workflow for users by converting their design submissions into a format suitable for chip manufacturing, ensuring compatibility and readiness for production.
