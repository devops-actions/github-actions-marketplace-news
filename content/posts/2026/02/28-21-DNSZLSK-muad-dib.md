## Action Summary

MUAD'DIB is a free and educational supply-chain security tool designed to detect and analyze threats in npm and PyPI dependencies. It combines static and dynamic analysis, deobfuscation, behavioral anomaly detection, and ground truth validation to identify malicious packages and provide actionable insights. By automating threat detection and response guidance, it helps developers quickly assess risk before installing dependencies, complementing enterprise security tools.
