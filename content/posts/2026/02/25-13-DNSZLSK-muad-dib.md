## Action Summary

MUAD'DIB is a security scanning tool designed to identify and respond to supply-chain threats in npm and PyPI dependencies. It combines static and dynamic analysis, deobfuscation, anomaly detection, and ground truth validation to detect malicious packages and suspicious patterns, offering actionable insights and risk scoring. This tool helps developers preemptively secure their projects by providing a free, fast, and educational first line of defense before installing unknown packages, complementing enterprise-grade solutions.
