## Action Summary

The `vercel-seo-audit` GitHub Action is a CLI tool designed to diagnose and explain why Google may not be indexing Next.js sites hosted on Vercel. It automates the detection of common SEO issues such as redirect loops, missing robots.txt or sitemaps, canonical URL mismatches, and misconfigurations related to Vercel or Next.js. This tool helps developers quickly identify and resolve technical SEO problems that can negatively impact search engine crawling and indexing.
