## Action Summary

VERIFRAX is a GitHub Action designed to enforce deterministic verification in CI pipelines, ensuring immutable and auditable governance boundaries. It automates cryptographic build attestation and certificate generation, providing high trust for regulated environments and preventing silent mutations or unauthorized changes. Its capabilities include fail-safe enforcement, forward-only governance, and independent offline verification of outcomes.
