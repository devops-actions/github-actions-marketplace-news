## Action Summary

The **execute-whitelist-action** GitHub Action is designed to control and whitelist specific workflow executions in a CI/CD pipeline. It automates the process of restricting runs to authorized or predefined conditions, helping to ensure secure and compliant execution of workflows. This action is particularly useful for maintaining control over workflow triggers and preventing unauthorized or unintended executions.
