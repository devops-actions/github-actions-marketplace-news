## Action Summary

NeuroLink is an enterprise-grade AI integration platform that unifies access to 13 major AI providers and over 100 models through a single, consistent API. It simplifies integrating AI into production applications by offering provider-agnostic features, automated cost optimization, failover support, and tools like Redis persistence and multi-provider routing. Available as a TypeScript SDK and CLI, it enables teams to develop, deploy, and scale AI-powered features efficiently.
