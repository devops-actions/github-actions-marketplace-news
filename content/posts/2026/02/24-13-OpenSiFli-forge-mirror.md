## Action Summary

Forge Mirror is an automation tool designed to synchronize repositories across GitHub, Gitee, GitLab, GitCode, and other Git services. It simplifies multi-platform repository management by automating tasks such as cloning, pushing, and maintaining repository structures, including branches, tags, and access configurations. With extensive customization options, it provides flexible rules for repository selection, renaming, visibility settings, and synchronization strategies, making it ideal for streamlining cross-platform collaboration and version control.
