## Action Summary

This GitHub Action provides advanced governance, risk, and compliance (GRC) capabilities for securing the software supply chain. It automates real-time enforcement of security policies, generates highly accurate SBOMs (Software Bill of Materials), detects hidden vulnerabilities, and mitigates modern threats like typosquatting and supply chain attacks. By implementing a zero-trust model, it offers continuous pipeline security, traffic inspection, and compliance automation, ensuring robust protection and streamlined operations for CI/CD environments.
