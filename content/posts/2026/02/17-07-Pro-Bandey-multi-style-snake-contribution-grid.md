## Action Summary

This GitHub Action generates dynamic, animated visualizations of your GitHub contribution graph in the form of snakes with customizable styles, shapes, and themes. It automates the process of creating multiple variations (e.g., different themes, geometric shapes, and month labels) and outputs high-quality SVG and GIF assets in a dedicated branch, along with a preview gallery file. This action simplifies the creation and display of personalized, visually appealing contribution charts for profiles or social sharing.
