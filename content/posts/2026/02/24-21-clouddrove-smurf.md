## Action Summary

Smurf is a command-line interface (CLI) tool built with Golang to simplify and automate workflows for DevOps tools like Terraform, Docker, and Helm. It provides unified commands to manage infrastructure, containers, and deployments, streamlining complex multi-tool operations and reducing the need for context-switching. Key capabilities include provisioning workflows, multi-cloud container registry support, and automatic credential management for enhanced productivity in DevOps environments.
