## Action Summary

The `tt-gds-action` GitHub Action automates the process of hardening Tiny Tapeout designs into manufacturable GDS files. It streamlines the conversion of Verilog-based designs into a format ready for fabrication, eliminating manual steps and reducing the potential for errors. This action is particularly useful for developers working with Tiny Tapeout projects, enabling efficient and consistent design finalization.
