## Action Summary

This GitHub Action transforms your GitHub contribution graph into animated visualizations with five unique styles, customizable shapes, and automated month labels. It automates the generation of high-quality SVG and GIF assets, providing a dynamic and customizable way to showcase your contribution activity. The action simplifies the process by auto-detecting the repository owner and generating a ready-to-use gallery in a dedicated branch for easy integration into your profile.
