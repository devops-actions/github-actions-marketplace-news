## Action Summary

The **Merge Guardian Workflow** GitHub Action integrates AI-powered analysis into CI/CD pipelines to predict potential merge conflicts and assess the risk levels of pull requests. It automates conflict detection and risk evaluation, helping teams proactively identify and resolve integration issues before merging. Key capabilities include AI-driven conflict prediction and support for multiple AI service providers, enhancing the reliability and efficiency of code collaboration workflows.
