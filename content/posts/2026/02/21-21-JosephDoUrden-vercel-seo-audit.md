## Action Summary

The `vercel-seo-audit` GitHub Action is a developer-friendly CLI tool designed to identify and explain SEO issues that prevent Google from properly indexing Next.js sites hosted on Vercel. It automates the detection of common misconfigurations such as redirect traps, missing robots.txt or sitemap files, noindex headers, and canonical mismatches, which can silently harm search engine rankings. By providing detailed reports and actionable insights, it helps developers optimize their sites for better crawling and indexing performance.
