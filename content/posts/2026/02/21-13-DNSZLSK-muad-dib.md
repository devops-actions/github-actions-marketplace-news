## Action Summary

MUAD'DIB is a security-focused GitHub Action designed to detect and analyze supply chain threats in npm and PyPI dependencies using static analysis, dynamic sandboxing, and behavioral anomaly detection. It automates the identification of known malicious packages and suspicious patterns, providing risk scores and actionable insights to help developers respond to threats before installation. This tool is ideal for developers seeking a free, quick, and educational solution to enhance package security in their projects.
