## Action Summary

The `ds-agent-rules` GitHub Action provides a portable and composable rules system to standardize AI coding practices across Data Science, Machine Learning, and AI Engineering projects. It automates the synchronization of predefined rulesets to multiple AI tools and platforms, ensuring alignment and consistency while preventing common issues like irreproducible experiments, data leakage, and untracked hyperparameters. This action simplifies rule management, enabling teams to maintain a single source of truth for their AI workflows.
