## Action Summary

Dependabit is a GitHub Action that leverages AI-powered language models to automatically discover, track, and monitor external dependencies in your codebase, including non-traditional dependencies like documentation, APIs, and research papers. It automates tasks such as dependency detection, categorization, change monitoring, and issue creation, enabling efficient updates and proactive management of external resources. By providing smart classification, confidence scoring, and customizable configurations, Dependabit simplifies and enhances dependency management for complex projects.
