## Action Summary

The `translation-toolkit` is a zero-dependency CLI tool designed for managing GNU gettext `.po` translation files efficiently. It automates tasks such as exporting `.po` files to CSV, JSON, or i18next formats for easy editing and web app integration, as well as importing updates back to `.po` files, including support for new languages. Additional capabilities include browser-based previews, validation for consistency, statistics reporting, and cross-format diffing, making it an all-in-one solution for streamlining translation workflows.
