## Action Summary

Shape is a GitHub Action and typechecker designed to enforce architectural conformance in software projects by providing a declarative, human-readable model using `.shape` files. It automates the validation of system design rules, such as resource constraints, function effects, structural relationships, and refactor-sensitive operations, ensuring consistency and coherence in architecture claims. Shape helps developers manage complex codebases by catching violations of declared rules, preventing unintended changes, and maintaining design integrity across updates and refactorings.
