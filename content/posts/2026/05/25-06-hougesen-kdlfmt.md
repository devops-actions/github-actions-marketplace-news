## Action Summary

`kdlfmt` is a command-line tool designed to format and validate [KDL](https://kdl.dev) documents, supporting both KDL version 1 and version 2. It automates the process of maintaining consistent formatting in KDL files by offering commands to format, check, and initialize formatter configurations, as well as generate shell completions. Built on top of the official Rust `kdl` parser, it ensures adherence to KDL specifications and simplifies the management of structured document formatting.
