## Action Summary

ZIRAN is an open-source security testing framework designed to identify vulnerabilities in AI agents, including those with tools, memory, and multi-step reasoning capabilities. It uses a graph-based model to analyze how an agent's capabilities interact, detecting dangerous tool chains, side effects, and multi-phase exploits that traditional single-prompt scanners may miss. Key features include tool-chain discovery, execution-level side-effect detection, and adaptive multi-phase campaign testing, offering comprehensive coverage of security benchmarks like OWASP and MITRE ATLAS.
