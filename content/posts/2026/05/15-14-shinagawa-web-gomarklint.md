## Action Summary

The **gomarklint** GitHub Action automates the validation and linting of Markdown files, identifying broken links, validating internal anchors, enforcing consistent heading structures, and ensuring clean and predictable document formatting. It is highly performant, capable of processing large files rapidly, and provides both human-readable and machine-friendly output, making it ideal for CI pipelines and quality assurance tasks.
