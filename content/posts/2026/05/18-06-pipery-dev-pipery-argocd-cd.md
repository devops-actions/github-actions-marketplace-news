## Action Summary

The **Pipery ArgoCD CD** GitHub Action automates the deployment of applications to ArgoCD, streamlining the process by updating container image tags, syncing applications, and monitoring rollout statuses with structured logging. It simplifies continuous delivery workflows by providing reusable steps for managing ArgoCD deployments, including support for custom configurations, resource pruning, and forced synchronization. This action is ideal for teams looking to integrate ArgoCD deployment into their CI/CD pipelines with minimal setup.
