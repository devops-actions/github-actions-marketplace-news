## Action Summary

AgentForge is an automated AI agent optimization platform designed to improve the quality of AI systems by iteratively testing, scoring, and refining their specifications. It eliminates manual testing by generating test scenarios, running agents, analyzing execution traces, and optimizing agent behavior until measurable improvements are achieved. Key capabilities include automated test generation, multi-dimensional scoring, iterative optimization, and integration with CI/CD pipelines via a CLI and REST API.
