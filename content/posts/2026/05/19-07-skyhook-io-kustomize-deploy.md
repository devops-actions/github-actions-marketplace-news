## Action Summary

The **Kustomize Deploy** GitHub Action automates Kubernetes deployments by supporting both GitOps workflows (e.g., ArgoCD) and direct `kubectl` deployments, with automatic detection of the appropriate method. It simplifies deployment tasks by updating Kubernetes manifests with image tags, labels, and environment variables, while also handling namespace creation and monitoring rollout status. Its key capabilities include dual deployment modes, multi-image support, and seamless integration with GitOps pipelines or direct Kubernetes management.
