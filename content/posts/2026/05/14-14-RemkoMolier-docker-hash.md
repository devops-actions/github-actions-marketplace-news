## Action Summary

`docker-hash` is a tool that computes a deterministic SHA-256 hash for a Docker image build based on the Dockerfile contents, build arguments, and files referenced by `COPY`/`ADD` instructions. It automates change detection, cache-busting, and ensures consistency for CI pipelines by generating unique hashes whenever relevant inputs are modified. This helps streamline workflows and maintain reliable build processes.
