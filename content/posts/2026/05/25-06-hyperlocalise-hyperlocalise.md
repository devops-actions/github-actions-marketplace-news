## Action Summary

The Hyperlocalise GitHub Action automates localization workflows by integrating translation quality checks and synchronization directly into CI pipelines. It enables tasks such as detecting localization changes, validating translation integrity, and annotating pull requests with insights about modified translation keys. This action streamlines the localization process, ensuring consistent and efficient management of multilingual content within software projects.
