## Action Summary

The **MCIX DataStage Overlay Apply GitHub Action** automates the process of applying one or more overlay directories to IBM DataStage export assets (zip or directory). It modifies base assets by overlaying changes such as updated JSON, additional assets, or property-driven substitutions, and outputs the updated assets to a specified location. This action streamlines the management and customization of DataStage assets, particularly in workflows involving environment-specific configurations or iterative updates.
