## Action Summary

Kyber is a Go code-quality analyzer that evaluates individual functions in a codebase against various metrics such as maintainability, complexity, readability, and testability. It provides detailed, per-function scores in human-readable, JSON, or SARIF formats, enabling developers to identify high-risk functions, track quality trends, and improve maintainability. Unlike traditional linters, Kyber focuses on function-level insights and supports easy integration of custom metrics for enhanced analysis.
