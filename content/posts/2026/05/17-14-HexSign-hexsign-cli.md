## Action Summary

The HexSign CLI is a tool designed to manage Apple developer signing assets, including certificates, provisioning profiles, devices, and identifiers, directly from the terminal or CI pipelines. It automates tasks such as authentication, asset provisioning, and secure credential handling, enabling streamlined integration with CI/CD workflows. Its key capabilities include zero-configuration operation, support for OAuth-based authentication, and advanced control over Apple signing materials.
