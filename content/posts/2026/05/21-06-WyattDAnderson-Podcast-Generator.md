## Action Summary

The **Podcast-Generator** GitHub Action automates the creation and management of podcast feeds by generating RSS files based on provided audio files and metadata. It simplifies the process of publishing podcasts by handling feed generation, ensuring compliance with podcasting standards, and eliminating the need for manual RSS editing. This action is ideal for streamlining podcast workflows and maintaining consistent, validated feed updates.
