## Action Summary

Swift-Build is a GitHub Action designed to automate the building and testing of Swift packages across multiple platforms, including macOS, iOS, Android, and Windows. It streamlines CI/CD workflows with intelligent caching, zero-configuration setup, and comprehensive platform coverage, optimizing performance for Swift development. The action solves common challenges in multi-platform Swift package management by providing platform-specific strategies and robust support for modern development pipelines.
