## Action Summary

Andy is a GitHub Action designed to streamline pull request (PR) reviews by automatically generating a concise, context-rich comment that explains the key changes, their purpose, and potential impact. It addresses common challenges such as understanding large diffs, inferring intent, and assessing the cost or value of changes, saving time for reviewers and improving team collaboration. Andy operates with minimal setup, requiring only a simple YAML file, and runs directly within your CI/CD pipeline without the need for external services.
