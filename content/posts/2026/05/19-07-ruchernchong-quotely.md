## Action Summary

Quotely is a GitHub Action that automates the daily generation and storage of AI-powered motivational quotes, complete with creative titles. It leverages a robust variety system to produce unique quotes, organizes them in both JSON and Markdown formats by date, and integrates smart duplicate handling. The action is reusable across repositories and workflows, making it ideal for automating content generation with minimal manual intervention.
