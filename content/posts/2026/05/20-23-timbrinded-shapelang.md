## Action Summary

Shape is a GitHub Action and typechecker designed to enforce and validate architectural conformance in software projects. It provides a structured language for defining architectural models, including resources, traits, components, and rules, and ensures these models are coherent by checking them against the source code. By automating the detection of issues like unauthorized effects, missing updates, and semantic violations, Shape helps maintain architectural integrity and supports safe refactoring.
