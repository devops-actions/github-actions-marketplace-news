## Action Summary

The **supply-chain-guard** GitHub Action is an open-source security scanner designed to detect and mitigate supply chain threats across various ecosystems, including npm, PyPI, Docker, Terraform, and GitHub repositories. It automates the detection of malware campaigns, code-level vulnerabilities, supply chain attacks, and infrastructure misconfigurations, while also verifying software integrity and generating accurate SBOMs. This action helps developers and organizations proactively secure their software supply chains by identifying over 170 threat indicators and correlating findings into actionable insights.
