## Action Summary

Manki is a self-hosted GitHub Action for AI-powered code reviews that adapts to your project's needs by assembling dynamic review teams based on pull request content and complexity. It automates code review tasks such as identifying critical issues, eliminating redundant feedback, and learning your team's coding conventions over time, while providing a conversational interface for collaboration. With capabilities like multi-stage pipelines, adaptive team sizing, and self-learning memory, Manki streamlines the review process, improves accuracy, and ensures control over your data by running on your own infrastructure.
