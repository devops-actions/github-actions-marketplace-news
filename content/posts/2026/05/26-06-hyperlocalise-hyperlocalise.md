## Action Summary

The **Hyperlocalise** GitHub Action streamlines localization workflows by automating CI checks for localization integrity and drift detection. It enables developers to integrate AI-driven localization directly into their engineering systems using a combination of a CLI, CI automation, and storage adapters. Key capabilities include executing localization checks, validating translation quality, and managing translation files across various formats, ultimately simplifying and improving the efficiency of localization processes.
