## Action Summary

The **AI Repo Digest** GitHub Action automates the generation of detailed, contributor-focused summaries of recent git activity within a repository. By utilizing a local Ollama AI model, it produces digestible insights about shipped changes, contributor contributions, and areas of expertise, enabling teams to stay informed about codebase updates without manually reviewing code. This action is ideal for improving collaboration and visibility across teams, while ensuring data privacy since no external services are used.
