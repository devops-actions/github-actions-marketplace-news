## Action Summary

The **gmetrics** GitHub Action is a Go-based implementation of the [lowlighter/metrics](https://github.com/lowlighter/metrics) tool, designed to generate SVG-based metrics visualizations. It automates the creation of customizable and visually appealing metrics dashboards, streamlining the process of tracking and displaying data insights.
