## Action Summary

The **blog-to-newsletter-action** automates the process of generating and sending newsletters for newly published blog posts. When a blog post is merged into the repository, it uses AI (via Claude) to create a newsletter draft, uploads it to Mailchimp, and notifies the user via Telegram for review and approval before sending. This action streamlines newsletter creation, integrates with third-party services, and offers a flexible, provider-based architecture for customization.
