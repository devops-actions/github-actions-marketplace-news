## Action Summary

Plumber is a compliance scanning tool designed to analyze CI/CD pipelines for GitLab CI and GitHub Actions. It automates the detection of security and policy violations by normalizing pipeline configurations into a provider-agnostic format, evaluating them against customizable Rego policies defined in a `.plumber.yaml` file. This action simplifies compliance management and ensures consistent enforcement of best practices across pipeline workflows.
