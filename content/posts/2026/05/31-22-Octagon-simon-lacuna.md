## Action Summary

Lacuna is a CLI tool that leverages AI to automate the process of improving test coverage in a codebase. It identifies untested code, generates and runs meaningful test cases, and retries or fixes failing tests, streamlining the process of achieving higher coverage with minimal manual intervention. This tool is particularly useful for developers looking to quickly identify coverage gaps, automate test creation, and ensure robust testing with reduced effort.
