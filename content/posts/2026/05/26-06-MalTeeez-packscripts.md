## Action Summary

The `packscripts` CLI tool is designed to streamline the management of modpacks by automating tasks such as mod organization, dependency visualization, version control, and packaging. It simplifies workflows by enabling users to initialize, update, enable/disable, and toggle mods, as well as manage remote versions and dependencies directly from the command line. This tool is particularly useful for developers or modpack creators looking to efficiently track, configure, and distribute their modpacks.
