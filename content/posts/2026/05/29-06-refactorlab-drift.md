## Action Summary

Andy is a GitHub Action that automates the process of reviewing pull requests by providing a single, comprehensive comment summarizing the changes, their context, and their potential impact. It addresses common challenges in code reviews, such as understanding the intent behind changes, assessing their implications, and reducing the time spent analyzing complex diffs. By offering clear, concise insights, Andy streamlines collaboration, minimizes misunderstandings, and improves the efficiency and quality of PR reviews.
