## Action Summary

This GitHub Action automates the process of purchasing Lotto 6/45 tickets from the Donghaeng Lottery platform in South Korea. It logs into the website, generates numbers, purchases tickets, and records the results in GitHub Issues, while also automatically checking for winnings after the draw. This action streamlines the ticket purchasing process and ensures consistent tracking of purchases and results.
