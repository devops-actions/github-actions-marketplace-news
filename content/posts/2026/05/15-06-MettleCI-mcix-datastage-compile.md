## Action Summary

The **MCIX DataStage Compile GitHub Action** automates the compilation of DataStage NextGen assets within a specified project, integrating seamlessly into CI/CD workflows. It leverages the `mcix datastage compile` command to streamline the process of compiling jobs or flows that have been imported into a DataStage project, reducing manual effort and ensuring efficient build pipelines. Key capabilities include project-specific compilation, audit tagging, and outputting the compile status for further validation.
