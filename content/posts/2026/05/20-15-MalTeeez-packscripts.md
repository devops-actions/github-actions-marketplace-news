## Action Summary

**Summary:**  
The `packscripts` GitHub Action is a command-line tool designed to streamline the management and versioning of modpacks. It automates tasks such as initializing mod folders, updating mod lists, toggling or enabling/disabling specific mods, visualizing dependencies, and packaging modpacks for distribution. By simplifying complex mod management workflows, it helps users efficiently track, update, and distribute their modpacks while reducing manual effort and errors.
