## Action Summary

The **Blackout Secure Marketplace Kit** is a comprehensive toolkit designed to streamline the development, validation, and publication of GitHub Marketplace Actions. It automates tasks such as pre-publish readiness checks, PR gating, action promotion, branding previews, and metadata synchronization to ensure compliance with Marketplace requirements and best practices. By catching issues early and enforcing rules, it helps developers avoid common publishing errors and simplifies the workflow for safely shipping and maintaining GitHub Actions.
