## Action Summary

This GitHub Action automates the process of counting words in markdown files within a repository, providing insights into total word counts per document, per section (based on configurable heading levels), and across the entire repository. It excludes specific content like image captions and generates detailed reports in CI logs and workflow summaries. This tool is valuable for tracking content word counts and analyzing text structure in markdown-based repositories.

## What's Changed

Counts words on your text files. Simple as that.
