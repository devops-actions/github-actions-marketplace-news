## Action Summary

This GitHub Action, **Review Insights**, analyzes pull request (PR) review activity within a repository to provide insights into workload distribution, merge outcomes, reviewer-author dynamics, and AI involvement. It automates the generation of visual reports, including heatmaps, bar charts, and time-series trends, to help identify review bottlenecks, workload imbalances, and the impact of AI on review and merge activity. By streamlining review analytics, it aids teams in optimizing collaboration and improving code review processes.
