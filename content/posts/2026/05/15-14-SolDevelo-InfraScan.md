## Action Summary

InfraScan is an open-source Infrastructure as Code (IaC) scanner designed to identify cost inefficiencies and security vulnerabilities in configurations for Terraform, Kubernetes, CloudFormation, Helm, and Dockerfiles. It automates the process of analyzing IaC for best practices, compliance, and risk reduction, making it suitable for use in development pipelines or standalone environments. Key features include multi-mode scanning (cost, security, and container vulnerabilities), professional reporting with actionable insights, and seamless integration with CI/CD workflows through a CLI or Docker image.
