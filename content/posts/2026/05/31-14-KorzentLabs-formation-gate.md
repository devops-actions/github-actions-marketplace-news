## Action Summary

Formation Gate is a diagnostic GitHub Action designed to verify that software-change claims are supported by evidence, such as passing tests or successful builds. It automates the generation of proof-only verification receipts, ensuring transparency and traceability without approving, merging, or deploying code. This action helps teams validate changes while maintaining a clear separation of diagnostic reporting from decision-making or automation tasks.
