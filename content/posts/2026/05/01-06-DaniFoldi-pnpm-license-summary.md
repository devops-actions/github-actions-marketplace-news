## Action Summary

The `pnpm-license-summary` GitHub Action generates a summary of licenses used in a pnpm project, helping developers identify and review license compliance within their dependencies. It automates the process of checking for allowed licenses and excluding specified packages, streamlining license management and ensuring adherence to project requirements.
