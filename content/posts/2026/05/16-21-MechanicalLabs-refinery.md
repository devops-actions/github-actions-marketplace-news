## Action Summary

The `refinery` GitHub Action is a high-performance build orchestrator designed to simplify and optimize CI/CD workflows, particularly for multi-platform projects. It automates the generation of platform-specific build workflows from a single, validated `refinery.toml` blueprint, eliminating the need for complex YAML configurations and reducing setup overhead. Key capabilities include native-first performance, cross-platform compatibility, type-safe configuration, and streamlined management of build logic and dependencies.
