## Action Summary

The `podcast-generator` GitHub Action automates the process of creating and publishing podcast RSS feeds. It simplifies podcast management by generating compliant RSS feeds from audio files, metadata, and episode details, eliminating the need for manual feed creation. This action is ideal for streamlining podcast publishing workflows and ensuring accurate RSS feed generation.
