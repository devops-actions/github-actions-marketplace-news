## Action Summary

Shape is a tool designed to enforce and validate architectural conformance in software projects by defining explicit, typed models in `.shape` files. It solves problems related to unclear or scattered system design by enabling checks on resources, components, dependencies, and function behaviors, ensuring coherence between declared architecture and implementation. Key capabilities include detecting violations of architectural rules (e.g., forbidden operations, dependency cycles), tracking changes for pull requests, and maintaining design context for refactor-sensitive functions.
