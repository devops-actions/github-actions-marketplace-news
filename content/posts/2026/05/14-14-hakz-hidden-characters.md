## Action Summary

The **Hidden Characters GitHub Action** scans files within a specified directory to detect hidden or non-printable characters, ensuring clean and readable codebases. It automates the process of identifying these characters and fails the workflow if any are found, helping maintain code quality and prevent issues caused by unintended characters.
