## Action Summary

**Summary:**  
The `packscripts` CLI tool is designed to streamline the management of modpacks by automating tasks such as mod tracking, version control, dependency visualization, and packaging. It simplifies the process of enabling, disabling, updating, and managing mod versions while providing tools to package and distribute modpacks efficiently. This action solves the challenge of manually handling complex mod dependencies and updates, making modpack development and maintenance more organized and less error-prone.
