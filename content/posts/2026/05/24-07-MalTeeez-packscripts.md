## Action Summary

"Packscripts" is a command-line tool designed to streamline the management of modpacks for games by automating tasks such as initializing mod folders, tracking and toggling mods, managing mod versions, and visualizing dependencies. It simplifies complex processes like enabling/disabling mods, checking for updates, and packaging modpacks for distribution. This tool is particularly useful for developers and gamers looking to efficiently organize, update, and deploy modpacks while maintaining version control.
