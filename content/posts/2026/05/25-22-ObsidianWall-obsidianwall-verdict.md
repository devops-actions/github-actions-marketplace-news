## Action Summary

ObsidianWall Verdict is a GitHub Action and CLI tool for evaluating Terraform plans against predefined governance policies before deployment. It automates policy enforcement by detecting budget overruns, compliance failures, and policy violations, providing a deterministic and auditable decision framework to prevent incidents. Key features include risk scoring, customizable policy conditions, decision overrides, and integration with CI/CD pipelines for automated deployment gating.
