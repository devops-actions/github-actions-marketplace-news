## Action Summary

Drift is an open-source performance and FinOps platform designed to detect, prevent, and resolve performance regressions early in the development lifecycle. It automates static call-graph analysis, runtime profiling, and per-PR performance evaluations to identify issues such as N+1 queries and blocking I/O, providing actionable insights directly on pull requests. By addressing performance bottlenecks during development, Drift reduces production incidents, cloud costs, and engineering overhead associated with late-stage fixes.
