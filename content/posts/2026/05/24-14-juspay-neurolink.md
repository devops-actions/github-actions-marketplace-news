## Action Summary

NeuroLink is a universal AI integration platform that consolidates over 21 AI providers and 100+ models into a single, consistent API, enabling seamless integration of AI capabilities into applications. It simplifies provider switching, optimizes costs through intelligent routing, and offers enterprise-grade features like multi-provider failover and Redis memory. By unifying AI streams (e.g., tokens, tools, memory, voice, documents), NeuroLink automates cross-provider interoperability, making AI deployment faster, more reliable, and scalable.
