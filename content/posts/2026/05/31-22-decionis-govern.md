## Action Summary

Decionis Action Gate is a GitHub Action designed to evaluate and enforce governance policies on high-risk actions before they execute. It automates the decision-making process for tasks like deployments, migrations, infrastructure changes, and AI-generated pull requests, providing verdicts to either allow, block, or escalate actions based on predefined policies and risk assessments. It also generates signed, audit-ready Decision Dossiers for transparency and compliance, ensuring organizations can monitor and control the execution of critical workflows effectively.
