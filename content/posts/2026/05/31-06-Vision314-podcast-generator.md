## Action Summary

The `podcast-generator` GitHub Action automates the creation and publishing of podcast RSS feeds. It streamlines the process of generating valid podcast metadata and episode entries, eliminating the need for manual feed management. This action is ideal for maintaining up-to-date podcast feeds directly from your repository.
