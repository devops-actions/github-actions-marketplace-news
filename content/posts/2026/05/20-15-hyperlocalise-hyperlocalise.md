## Action Summary

The `Hyperlocalise` GitHub Action automates localization workflows by integrating AI-powered tools into your CI pipeline. It enables tasks such as detecting localization drift, validating translation integrity, and generating focused annotations for pull request changes, streamlining the process of managing translations within an engineering system. This action eliminates the need for external localization workflows, ensuring seamless integration with your development process.
