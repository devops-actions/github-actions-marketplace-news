## Action Summary

The ANCHOR Protocol is a decentralized software supply chain integrity solution built on the Algorand blockchain. It enables developers to cryptographically anchor the hash of a software artifact to their own Algorand wallet, creating a tamper-evident, timestamped record that downstream consumers can independently verify without relying on intermediaries or centralized services. This action addresses the risks of supply chain attacks by providing a self-sovereign, permissionless, and transparent verification mechanism for software integrity.
