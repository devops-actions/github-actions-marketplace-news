## Action Summary

Lacuna is an AI-powered CLI tool designed to improve test coverage in codebases by automating the analysis, generation, and verification of tests. It identifies untested code, creates meaningful test cases, runs them, and retries fixes for failing tests—all in a single command. This action streamlines the process of writing and maintaining tests, helping developers close coverage gaps efficiently while ensuring their codebase remains robust and error-free.
