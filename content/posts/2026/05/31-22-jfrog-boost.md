## Action Summary

Boost is a GitHub Action and command-line tool designed to enhance coding agents, shell commands, and CI pipelines by intelligently filtering and compressing logs. It minimizes token usage by preserving essential information (e.g., errors, timings, cache hits) while removing repetitive or noisy outputs, enabling faster and more efficient workflows. Boost supports a wide range of agents, CI systems, and commands, and simplifies the debugging and monitoring process with structured and actionable log summaries.
