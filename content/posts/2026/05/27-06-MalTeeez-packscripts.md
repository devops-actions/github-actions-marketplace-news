## Action Summary

The `packscripts` CLI tool is designed for managing and automating tasks related to modpack development, particularly those tracked using Git. It streamlines processes such as initializing and maintaining mod lists, enabling/disabling mods, tracking remote versions, verifying source links, and packaging modpacks for distribution. This tool simplifies modpack version control, dependency management, and update workflows, making it ideal for developers looking to efficiently organize and maintain their projects.
