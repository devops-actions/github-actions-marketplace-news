## Action Summary

The **Lineaje Scan Action** automates vulnerability scanning for container images and source code, providing a summary of security issues detected. It can also generate and apply fix plans, producing patched artifacts such as updated Dockerfiles or dependency manifests, streamlining remediation workflows. This action simplifies security management by integrating scanning, fixing, and verification processes directly into CI/CD pipelines.
