## Action Summary

The **Naukri Resume Action** is a GitHub Action that automates the process of uploading resumes to Naukri.com, ensuring your profile remains fresh and active without manual intervention. It supports scheduled and manual uploads, secure credential handling, and allows the use of multiple resume files. This action simplifies the task of keeping your Naukri profile updated, saving time and increasing visibility to potential employers.
