## Action Summary

ActionScope is a static analysis tool designed to evaluate the security of GitHub Actions workflows, particularly their interaction with AWS resources. It identifies potential vulnerabilities such as compromised actions, misconfigured OIDC trust policies, unpinned actions, and risks related to script injection or artifact poisoning, providing clear, human-readable reports on the blast radius of compromised credentials. By automating the risk assessment process, it helps developers secure their CI/CD pipelines without requiring AWS credentials or extensive manual analysis.
