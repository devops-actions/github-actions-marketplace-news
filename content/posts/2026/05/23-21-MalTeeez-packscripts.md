## Action Summary

**Packscripts** is a command-line tool designed to help manage and streamline the development and maintenance of modpacks, particularly for games. It automates tasks such as initializing and organizing mod folders, enabling or disabling specific mods, tracking and updating mod versions, and packaging modpacks for distribution. Its key capabilities include dependency visualization, version management, and efficient packaging of modpacks with update support, reducing manual effort and improving workflow efficiency for modpack creators.
