## Action Summary

**HF-MS Sync** is a GitHub Action designed to automate the bidirectional synchronization of AI models and datasets between HuggingFace Hub and ModelScope. It streamlines the management of repositories across these platforms by detecting changes, resolving conflicts, and supporting large files, all while offering flexible configuration options. This action reduces manual effort, ensures consistency, and provides detailed sync reports for improved workflow visibility.
