## Action Summary

This GitHub Action integrates SCAL-P into your CI workflows to enforce dependency policies, verify package integrity, and audit JavaScript projects. It automates tasks such as resolving lockfiles, blocking policy violations, validating dependency hashes, and generating structured JSON/SARIF reports for security and compliance analysis. By prioritizing policy enforcement and hash verification, it helps mitigate risks associated with arbitrary code execution during dependency installation.
