## Action Summary

`agent-bom` is a security scanning and compliance tool for AI and MCP (Model Control Plane) infrastructure. It automates the discovery and analysis of vulnerabilities, dependencies, and exposure paths across agents, servers, tools, and environments, providing actionable findings and compliance evidence. Its key capabilities include graph-based inventory generation, runtime controls, and integration with CLI, REST API, and a self-hosted dashboard for comprehensive visibility.
