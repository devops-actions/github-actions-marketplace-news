## Action Summary

This GitHub Action, *Prechained Supply Chain Verify*, automates the verification of project dependencies against the Prechained cryptographic archive, a Bitcoin-anchored record of software supply chain data. It ensures that dependencies used in CI/CD pipelines are clean and predate any supply chain attacks, providing cryptographically anchored forensic proof of their integrity. Key capabilities include scanning npm, PyPI, and Cargo packages, generating verification reports, and identifying missing or unverified packages.
