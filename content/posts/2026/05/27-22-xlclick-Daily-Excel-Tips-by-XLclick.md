## Action Summary

This GitHub Action, **Daily Excel Tips by XLClick**, automates the delivery of daily Excel productivity tips to a specified Telegram chat or channel every morning at 09:00 UTC. It cycles through 34 preloaded tips stored in the repository, ensuring no duplicates are sent on the same day and no immediate repeats within a cycle. The action eliminates the need for external API calls (other than Telegram) and tracks its state to provide consistent, automated communication and improve users' Excel skills.
