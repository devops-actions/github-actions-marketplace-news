## Action Summary

The **Black Duck Notice Generator GitHub Action** automates the creation, management, and modification of license notice files for projects integrated with Black Duck. It streamlines tasks such as generating new notice files, downloading the latest versions, and cleaning up or modifying existing files, ensuring compliance with open-source license requirements. This action is particularly useful for automating license management workflows directly within CI/CD pipelines.
