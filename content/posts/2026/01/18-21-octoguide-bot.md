## Action Summary

OctoGuide is a GitHub Action designed to ensure contributors follow best practices within a repository. It automates the process of monitoring contributor actions and provides friendly feedback when actions deviate from expected workflows, helping maintain smooth project management and collaboration. This tool reduces manual oversight and fosters a productive development environment.
