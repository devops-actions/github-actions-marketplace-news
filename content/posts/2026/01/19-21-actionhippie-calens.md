## Action Summary

The `calens` GitHub Action automates the generation of changelogs using predefined templates and changelog definitions. It simplifies the process of creating and updating changelogs for repositories, ensuring consistent documentation of changes across versions. Key capabilities include generating changelogs for specific versions, customizing output formats, and directly writing or printing the changelog content.
