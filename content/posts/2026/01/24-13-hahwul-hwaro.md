Hwaro is a lightweight and fast static site generator written in Crystal, designed to simplify the creation and management of static websites. It automates the process of transforming source files into optimized static assets, enabling developers to quickly build and deploy static sites. Its key capabilities include high performance and ease of use for site generation tasks.
