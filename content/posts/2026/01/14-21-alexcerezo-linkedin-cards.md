## Action Summary

The GitHub Readme LinkedIn Card Generator is a GitHub Action that automates the creation and daily updating of visually appealing LinkedIn post cards, which can be displayed in your GitHub profile README. It streamlines the process of showcasing your latest LinkedIn activity, offering features like light and dark themes, responsive SVG cards, and multi-language support. This tool is ideal for professionals who want to seamlessly integrate their LinkedIn presence into their GitHub profile.

## Release notes

# Video Posts Support
Your GitHub profile just got more dynamic! We are excited to announce that `linkedin-cards` now supports **Video Posts**.
Previously, video posts might have been skipped or rendered incorrectly. Now, the action detects them and generates a specific SVG card layout, capturing the essence of your video content to display proudly on your README.
