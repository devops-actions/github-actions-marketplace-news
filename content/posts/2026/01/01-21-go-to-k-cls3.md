## Action Summary

The `cls3` CLI tool simplifies the process of managing Amazon S3 buckets by providing functionality to efficiently empty, delete, or manage multiple buckets, including those with versioning enabled. It supports cross-region operations, advanced search capabilities for bucket names, selective deletion of old object versions, and compatibility with S3-compatible storage via custom endpoints. This tool is ideal for automating and streamlining S3 bucket cleanup and management tasks.
