## Action Summary

QWED-Finance is a middleware designed to ensure the mathematical accuracy and compliance of AI-generated financial calculations before they are used in production. It automates the verification of key financial computations such as NPV, IRR, loan amortization, and derivatives pricing, while also addressing compliance with regulatory standards and preventing errors like floating-point inaccuracies in currency calculations. This tool provides robust validation capabilities for banking and financial systems, enhancing reliability and trust in AI-driven outputs.
