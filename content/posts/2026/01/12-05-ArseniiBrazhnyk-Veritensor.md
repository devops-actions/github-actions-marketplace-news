## Action Summary

Veritensor is a security platform designed to ensure the safety, authenticity, compliance, and trustworthiness of AI models within the supply chain. It automates deep static analysis, cryptographic verification, and license compliance checks for AI formats such as Pickle and PyTorch, detecting malicious code, unauthorized modifications, and restricted licenses. Additionally, it integrates seamlessly with CI/CD pipelines and tools like Sigstore Cosign to enhance supply chain security through cryptographic signing and tamper prevention.
