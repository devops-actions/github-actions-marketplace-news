## Action Summary

This GitHub Action facilitates the automated upload of comma-delimited files (CSV) or directories containing such files to projects hosted on [biglocalnews.org](https://biglocalnews.org). It streamlines the process of transferring data for journalism or research purposes, saving time and reducing manual effort. The action is particularly useful for integrating data uploads into CI/CD workflows for collaborative data projects.
