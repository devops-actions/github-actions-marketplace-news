## Action Summary

The **Deno Outdated Action** automates the process of checking and updating outdated dependencies in Deno projects. It identifies outdated dependencies, creates a new branch, updates the dependencies, and generates a pull request with the changes, streamlining dependency management. This action helps maintain up-to-date and secure Deno projects with minimal manual effort.
