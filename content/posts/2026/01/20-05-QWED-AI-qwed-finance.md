## Action Summary

QWED-Finance is a middleware tool designed to verify and ensure the accuracy of AI-generated financial calculations for banking and finance applications. It automates the validation of critical computations, such as NPV, IRR, loan amortization, and derivatives pricing, while preventing errors in currency calculations and ensuring compliance with regulatory standards. By providing deterministic verification for financial outputs, it enhances accuracy, regulatory compliance, and trust in AI-driven financial systems.
