## Action Summary

The `semantic-issue-action` is a GitHub Action designed to ensure issue titles in a repository comply with the Conventional Commits specification. It automates the process by validating issue titles when they are created, edited, or reopened, and posts comments if the titles do not meet the required format. This action helps maintain consistent and meaningful issue titles, improving organization and clarity in repositories.
