## Action Summary

The "Assign/Unassign Issue Action" is a GitHub Action that automates the management of issue assignments through simple comment commands (`/assign` and `/unassign`). It streamlines issue tracking by enforcing assignment limits, sending reminder notifications for pending tasks, and automatically unassigning inactive issues after a configurable period. This action helps teams improve task management and accountability while ensuring issues are actively worked on.
