## Action Summary

NeuroLink is an enterprise-grade AI integration platform that unifies access to 13 major AI providers and over 100 models through a consistent API, enabling seamless provider switching and cost optimization. It simplifies AI development with a TypeScript SDK and CLI, offering production-ready features such as multi-provider failover, Redis-based persistence, and built-in tools for enhanced functionality. NeuroLink automates complex AI workflows, making it easier for teams to build, deploy, and scale AI-powered applications efficiently.
