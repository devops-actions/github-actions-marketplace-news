## Action Summary

The `podcast-generator` GitHub Action automates the generation of podcast files and commits the changes to a repository. It streamlines the process of creating and managing podcast content, ensuring consistent updates with minimal manual intervention. The action supports customization of commit author details to suit specific workflows.
