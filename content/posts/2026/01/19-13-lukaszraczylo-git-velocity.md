## Action Summary

Git Velocity is a GitHub Action designed to analyze repository activity and generate a gamified dashboard showcasing developer velocity metrics. It automates the tracking of contributions such as commits, pull requests, code reviews, and issues while incorporating a scoring system, achievements, and leaderboards to foster engagement and competition. With its powerful analytics, team support, and visually appealing dashboard, it helps developers and teams monitor productivity and identify trends in their development workflows.
