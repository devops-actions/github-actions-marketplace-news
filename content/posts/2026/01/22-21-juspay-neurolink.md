## Action Summary

NeuroLink is an enterprise AI integration platform that simplifies the development and deployment of AI features by providing a unified API for 13 major AI providers and over 100 models. It streamlines provider switching, automates cost optimization, and offers production-ready capabilities like multi-provider failover, Redis persistence, and intelligent routing. NeuroLink enables teams to efficiently build and operate AI-driven applications through its TypeScript SDK and professional CLI, solving the complexities of managing diverse AI tools and infrastructure.
