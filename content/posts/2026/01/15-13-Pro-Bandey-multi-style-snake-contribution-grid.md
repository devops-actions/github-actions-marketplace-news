## Action Summary

The "Multi-Style Snake Contribution Grid" GitHub Action transforms your GitHub contribution graph into dynamic, animated visuals with five customizable styles, unique shapes, and bold month labels. It automates the generation of high-quality SVG and GIF animations, creates a gallery of styles in an output branch, and simplifies user setup with auto-detection of the repository owner. This action is ideal for showcasing visually engaging contribution graphs on profiles or for social sharing.
