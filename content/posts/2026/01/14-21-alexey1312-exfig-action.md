## Action Summary

ExFig Action is a GitHub Action designed to automate the export of design assets—such as colors, icons, images, and typography—from Figma to a codebase using the ExFig CLI. It streamlines the integration of Figma assets into development workflows by providing built-in caching for incremental exports and supporting granular configuration options. This action saves time and reduces manual effort in asset management while ensuring consistency across projects.
