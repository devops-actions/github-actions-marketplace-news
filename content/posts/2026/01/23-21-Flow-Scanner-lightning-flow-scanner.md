## Action Summary

The Lightning Flow Scanner is a tool designed to analyze and optimize Salesforce Flows by detecting unsafe practices, anti-patterns, and potential issues such as queries in loops, hardcoded IDs, and insecure contexts. It automates the identification of problematic configurations and provides actionable insights to improve Flow performance, security, and maintainability. Its key capabilities include rule-based scanning, customizable configurations, and support for CI/CD integration.
