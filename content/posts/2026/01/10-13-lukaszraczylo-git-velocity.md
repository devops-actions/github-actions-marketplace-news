## Action Summary

Git Velocity is a GitHub Action that analyzes your repositories to generate a gamified dashboard with detailed developer velocity metrics, such as commits, pull requests, reviews, and issues. It automates the process of tracking contributions, visualizing activity trends, and providing team analytics, while incorporating gamification features like achievements, leaderboards, and scoring systems. This action helps developers and teams monitor productivity, identify patterns, and foster engagement through interactive and visually appealing insights.
