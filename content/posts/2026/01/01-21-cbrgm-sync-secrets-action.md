## Action Summary

The **Sync Secrets Action** is a GitHub Action designed to automate the synchronization of repository, Dependabot, and Codespaces secrets and variables across multiple GitHub repositories and environments. It simplifies secret and variable management by enabling users to replicate, update, and maintain consistency of these configurations programmatically, including batch processing through search queries. This action addresses challenges in managing secrets across multiple repositories, reducing manual effort and minimizing the risk of inconsistencies or errors.
