## Action Summary

This GitHub Action, **action-crosspost**, automates the process of crossposting content from a sitemap or RSS/Atom feed to multiple social media platforms (e.g., Twitter/X, Mastodon, LinkedIn). It filters posts based on age or URL patterns, supports customizable message templates, and allows for dry-run mode to preview posts before publishing. This action simplifies content distribution and ensures timely and consistent posting across various networks.
