## Action Summary

NeuroLink is an enterprise AI integration platform that unifies access to 13 major AI providers and over 100 models under a consistent API, enabling seamless development and deployment of AI-powered applications. It simplifies provider switching, automates cost optimization, and offers advanced features like Redis persistence, multi-provider failover, and built-in tools for production-ready AI workflows. With both a TypeScript SDK and a CLI, it accelerates development while ensuring scalability and reliability for enterprise use cases.
