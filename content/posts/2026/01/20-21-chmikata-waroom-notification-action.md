## Action Summary

The **waroom-notification-action** is a GitHub Action designed to automate the process of retrieving incident information from Waroom and sending notifications to Slack. It streamlines incident management by leveraging Slack's incoming webhooks to share critical updates, ensuring teams are promptly informed of incidents. This action is ideal for enhancing communication and incident awareness within DevOps workflows.
