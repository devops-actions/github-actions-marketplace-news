## Action Summary

The `ansible-later` GitHub Action is designed to lint and validate Ansible roles, ensuring adherence to best practices and standards. It automates the process of inspecting Ansible files for issues, streamlining quality checks and reducing manual effort during development. Key capabilities include configurable rule sets, filtering, exclusions, and adjustable logging levels for enhanced control and visibility.
