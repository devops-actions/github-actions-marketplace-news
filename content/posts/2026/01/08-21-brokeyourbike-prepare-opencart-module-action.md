## Action Summary

The `prepare-opencart-module-action` GitHub Action automates the packaging of files into a ZIP archive that complies with the requirements for OpenCart modules. It simplifies the process of preparing module files for distribution and ensures they are correctly structured, addressing the common challenge of manual packaging errors. Key capabilities include support for adding modification and license files, as well as specifying directories or files to include in the archive.
