## Action Summary

NeuroLink is an enterprise-grade AI integration platform that unifies access to 13 major AI providers and 100+ models under a consistent API. It streamlines AI development by enabling seamless provider switching, automating cost optimization, and offering robust production-ready features such as Redis persistence, multi-provider failover, and intelligent routing. Designed for scalability, it supports rapid iteration and deployment of AI-powered features through its TypeScript SDK and professional CLI.
