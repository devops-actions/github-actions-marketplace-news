## Action Summary

The Changebot Notify Deployment Action is a GitHub Action designed to automate notifications to Changebot about deployment events. It streamlines the process of generating customer-facing product updates by integrating deployment details directly into the Changebot platform. This action eliminates the need for manual updates, improves workflow efficiency, and ensures accurate tracking of deployment activities.
