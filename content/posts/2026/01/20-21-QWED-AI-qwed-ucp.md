## Action Summary

QWED-UCP is a verification tool designed for ensuring the mathematical accuracy and compliance of AI-driven e-commerce transactions using Google's Universal Commerce Protocol (UCP). It addresses issues like AI-generated errors in calculations, tax percentages, and schema compliance by employing three distinct verification engines: SymPy for financial computations, Z3 for state machine logic, and JSON Schema for UCP structure validation. This tool automates the process of validating transactions, providing a reliable safeguard against errors in AI-mediated commerce.
