## Action Summary

The "Bantuu Veracode Baseline" GitHub Action automates the integration of Veracode's security scanning tools, such as the Pipeline Scan and Auto Packager, with optional support for Bantuu baselines. It streamlines the process of generating and scanning application artifacts (`.zip`), optionally leveraging Bantuu baselines for enhanced tracking and policy enforcement. This Action is designed to simplify security scans in CI/CD workflows, supporting various scenarios with flexible configurations.
