## Action Summary

The **healthchecks-action** GitHub Action integrates with Healthchecks.io to monitor the status of scheduled jobs and workflows. It automates the process of sending pings to Healthchecks.io at the start and end of a job, providing real-time updates on job execution success or failure. This helps users ensure the reliability and health of their workflows by automating failure detection and status tracking.
