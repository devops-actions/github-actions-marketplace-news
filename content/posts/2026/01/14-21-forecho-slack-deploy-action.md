## Action Summary

The Slack Deploy Action is a reusable GitHub Action designed to send deployment notifications to a specified Slack channel in a compact, attachment-style format. It automates the process of notifying teams about deployment updates, supporting both English and Chinese languages for flexibility. Key capabilities include customizable notification details such as service name, color, and footer text, streamlining communication during deployments.
