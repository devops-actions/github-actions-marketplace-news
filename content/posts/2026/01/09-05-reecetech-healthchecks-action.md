## Action Summary

The `healthchecks-action` GitHub Action integrates with Healthchecks.io to monitor the status of scheduled jobs and workflows. It automates the process of sending start and end pings to a specified Healthchecks.io URL, providing real-time updates on whether a job has succeeded or failed. This action helps ensure visibility into workflow health and simplifies tracking and debugging of job execution.
