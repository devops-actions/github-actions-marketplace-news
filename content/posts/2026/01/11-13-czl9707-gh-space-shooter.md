## Action Summary

The `gh-space-shooter` GitHub Action transforms your GitHub contribution graph into an animated space shooter game, visualizing your coding history as enemies in a Galaga-style battle. It automates the generation and daily update of a game GIF, allowing users to showcase dynamic, visually engaging representations of their contributions. This action simplifies the process of creating and maintaining the game, offering customization options like attack strategies, frame rates, and output formats.
