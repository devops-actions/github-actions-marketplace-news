## Action Summary

The NetDiag Health Check GitHub Action automates post-deployment network diagnostics by verifying the health and connectivity of endpoints across multiple global regions. It performs comprehensive checks including DNS resolution, TLS certificate validation, HTTP response, and ICMP ping, providing actionable outputs such as overall health status and detailed JSON results for further processing. This action helps ensure services are functioning correctly after deployment and simplifies monitoring tasks with multi-region insights.
