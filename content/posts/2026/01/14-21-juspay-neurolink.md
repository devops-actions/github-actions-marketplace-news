## Action Summary

NeuroLink is an enterprise-grade AI integration platform that unifies multiple AI providers and models under a single, consistent API, enabling seamless integration of AI capabilities into applications. It simplifies switching between providers, optimizes costs with intelligent routing, and offers tools like multi-provider failover, Redis persistence, and built-in AI utilities. Designed for production at scale, it provides both a TypeScript SDK and CLI to accelerate development, deployment, and management of AI-powered features.
