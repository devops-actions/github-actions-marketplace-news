## Action Summary

The `kusari-ingest` GitHub Action automates the ingestion of various artifacts, such as SBOMs (Software Bill of Materials) and attestations like SLSA, into the Kusari Platform as part of a CI/CD workflow. It simplifies the integration of artifact data into Kusari, enabling users to efficiently manage and query security and provenance information within their tenant environment. This action helps streamline artifact ingestion and verification processes, supporting enhanced software supply chain security.
