## Action Summary

The **Self-Hosted Runner Job Cleaner** GitHub Action automates the cleanup of a self-hosted runner's workspace at the end of a job, ensuring that leftover files are removed. It solves the problem of accumulated files causing disk space issues or workflow errors in persistent runners. Key capabilities include automatic post-job cleanup, reducing manual intervention, and ensuring consistent runner workspace hygiene.
