## Action Summary

This GitHub Action generates a dynamic, animated visualization of your GitHub contribution graph in various styles, shapes, and themes, such as light, dark, and hacker modes. It automates the creation of multiple snake animations with distinct designs, adds bold month labels for better context, and outputs high-quality SVG and GIF files for sharing or embedding. The action simplifies the process by auto-detecting the repository owner and generating a gallery of assets in a dedicated branch.
