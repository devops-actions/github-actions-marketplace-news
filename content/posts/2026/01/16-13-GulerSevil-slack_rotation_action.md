## Action Summary

The **Slack Goalie Rotation GitHub Action** automates the process of managing on-call rotations for a team by updating a rotation file, notifying Slack channels, and managing Slack user groups. It supports various rotation modes and cadences, streamlining communication and ensuring consistent updates to Slack topics or messages about the current goalie and deputy. This action eliminates manual updates, enhances team organization, and integrates seamlessly with Slack for efficient on-call management.
