## Action Summary

NeuroLink is an enterprise AI integration platform that unifies access to 13 major AI providers and over 100 models through a consistent API. It simplifies AI development by enabling seamless provider switching, cost optimization, and failover capabilities, while offering production-grade tools like Redis persistence and multi-provider routing. Delivered as a TypeScript SDK and CLI, it automates AI feature development, deployment, and scaling for robust, efficient, and flexible applications.
