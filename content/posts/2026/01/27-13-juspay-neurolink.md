## Action Summary

NeuroLink is an enterprise AI integration platform that provides a unified API to access 13 major AI providers and over 100 models, enabling seamless integration of AI capabilities into production applications. It simplifies provider switching, automates cost optimization, and offers tools for failover, Redis memory persistence, and intelligent routing. With its TypeScript SDK and CLI, NeuroLink accelerates AI feature development while ensuring scalability, reliability, and efficiency for enterprise use cases.
