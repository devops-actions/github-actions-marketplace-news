## Action Summary

Hwaro is a lightweight and fast static site generator written in Crystal, designed for building high-performance websites using Markdown and Jinja2-compatible templates. It automates tasks such as content processing, layout management, and metadata generation (e.g., Sitemaps and RSS feeds) while offering features like parallel processing and caching for improved performance. Hwaro simplifies the creation and management of blogs, documentation, and personal project sites through a streamlined workflow and built-in SEO support.
