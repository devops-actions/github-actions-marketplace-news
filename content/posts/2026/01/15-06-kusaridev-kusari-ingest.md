## Action Summary

The `kusari-ingest` GitHub Action automates the ingestion of software artifacts, such as SBOMs, SLSA attestations, and other compliance-related documents, into the Kusari Platform directly from your CI/CD workflows. It simplifies artifact management and integration with the Kusari ecosystem, enabling efficient tracking, analysis, and compliance verification with minimal manual input. Key capabilities include authentication, artifact tagging, dependency checks, and real-time ingestion status monitoring.
