## Action Summary

Lingo.dev is an open-source AI-powered internationalization (i18n) toolkit designed to simplify and automate the process of making applications multilingual. The Lingo.dev Compiler specifically enables build-time localization for React applications without modifying existing components, allowing seamless generation of language-specific bundles. It addresses the challenges of managing translations by integrating with CI/CD pipelines, offering a CLI for one-command localization, and supporting real-time translation for dynamic content.
