## Action Summary

NeuroLink is an enterprise AI integration platform that unifies access to 13 major AI providers and over 100 models through a single, consistent API. It simplifies AI development with a TypeScript SDK and CLI by enabling seamless provider switching, intelligent cost optimization, and enterprise-grade features like Redis persistence and multi-provider failover. Designed for production-scale applications, it automates AI integration, tooling, and deployment, reducing complexity and accelerating development workflows.
