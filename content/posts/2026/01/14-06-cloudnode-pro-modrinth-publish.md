## Action Summary

The `modrinth-publish` GitHub Action automates the process of publishing project versions to Modrinth, a platform for hosting Minecraft mods, plugins, and other content. It simplifies version management by automating tasks such as uploading files, specifying loaders, and setting version metadata. This action is particularly useful for developers seeking to streamline their release workflows and ensure consistency in version deployment.
