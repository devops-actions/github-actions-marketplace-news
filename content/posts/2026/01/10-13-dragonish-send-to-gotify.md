## Action Summary

The `send-to-gotify` GitHub Action allows users to send customizable notifications to a Gotify server directly from their workflows. It automates the task of delivering messages with various attributes, such as priority, title, or media links, providing a streamlined way to integrate notification capabilities into CI/CD pipelines. This action is ideal for keeping teams informed of build statuses, deployment updates, or other workflow events.
