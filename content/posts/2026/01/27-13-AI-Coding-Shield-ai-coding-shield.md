## Action Summary

AI Coding Shield is a security auditing tool designed for AI development workflows, skills, rules, and Model Context Protocol (MCP) servers. It automates the detection of potential risks such as command injection, data exfiltration, insecure configurations, and obfuscated code, helping developers identify vulnerabilities in AI-assisted environments. By providing flexible, rule-based threat detection and customizable reporting, it enhances security in CI/CD pipelines and AI development processes.
