## Action Summary

The **MCPB Pack** GitHub Action automates the packaging of MCP servers into portable `.mcpb` bundles and optionally announces them to the [mpak registry](https://www.mpak.dev) for public discovery. It simplifies workflows by handling the building of bundles with vendored dependencies, supporting flexible modes for building, announcing, or both. This action is ideal for streamlining the distribution and registration of MCP server packages.
