## Action Summary

The **hiero-solo-action** GitHub Action automates the setup of a temporary Hiero Solo network for testing or development purposes. It provisions one or two consensus nodes, optionally installs a mirror node with a REST API, and creates a funded account with a configurable hbar balance. This action simplifies the process of creating isolated blockchain environments, enabling efficient CI/CD workflows for Hedera-based projects.
