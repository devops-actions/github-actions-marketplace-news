## Action Summary

Totem is a CLI tool designed to provide AI coding agents with a persistent memory and enforcement layer to prevent recurring mistakes and maintain architectural integrity across projects. It automates the conversion of plain-English lessons into enforceable rules that block problematic code patterns during critical operations, such as `git push`, ensuring consistent adherence to best practices without relying on runtime LLMs. This solves the challenge of stateless AI agents forgetting context and helps streamline code reviews by proactively catching issues before they are introduced.
