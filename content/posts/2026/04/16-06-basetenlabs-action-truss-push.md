## Action Summary

The **Truss Push Action** automates the deployment of machine learning models or chains to Baseten, handling tasks such as pushing deployments, monitoring activation status, and optional validation with prediction requests. It simplifies workflows by detecting models or chains based on the provided directory, ensuring deployments are active and validated without manual intervention. Key capabilities include customizable deployment settings, environment targeting, and integration with CI pipelines for streamlined model or chain management.
