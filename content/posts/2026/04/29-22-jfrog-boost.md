## Action Summary

Boost is a versatile tool designed to speed up command execution and streamline output across three key areas: terminal commands, coding agents, and continuous integration (CI) pipelines. It reduces unnecessary log noise, enhances performance through caching, and provides detailed telemetry using OpenTelemetry for better traceability and diagnostics. By acting as a single, consistent binary, Boost simplifies workflows and improves efficiency for developers and automated systems.
