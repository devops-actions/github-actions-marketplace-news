**OLIVE Action** automates open-source license compliance by integrating with the OLIVE CLI and platform. It performs dependency analysis on pull requests, generates comments with the results, and uploads detailed reports as artifacts, enabling efficient license and vulnerability management. This action simplifies compliance workflows by automating analysis, reporting, and data integration with the OLIVE Platform.
