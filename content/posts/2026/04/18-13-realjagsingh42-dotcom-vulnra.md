## Action Summary

VULNRA is a GitHub Action that automates security scanning of Large Language Model (LLM) APIs to identify vulnerabilities such as prompt injections, jailbreaks, data leakage, and compliance violations. By integrating into CI/CD pipelines, it provides risk scores, detailed categorized findings, and compliance mappings to frameworks like OWASP LLM Top 10 and the EU AI Act, helping teams proactively address security risks in AI systems. This action streamlines vulnerability detection, enforces risk thresholds, and ensures regulatory alignment in AI deployments.
