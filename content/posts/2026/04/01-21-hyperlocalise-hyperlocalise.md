## Action Summary

The Hyperlocalise GitHub Action automates localization workflows by integrating AI-powered translation processes directly into CI pipelines. It provides key functionalities such as detecting localization changes (`drift`) and ensuring localization integrity (`check`) with actionable reports and artifact uploads. This action streamlines translation management within the development workflow, reducing manual effort and improving localization efficiency.
