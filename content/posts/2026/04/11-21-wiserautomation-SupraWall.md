## Action Summary

SupraWall is a security-focused GitHub Action designed to protect AI agents by intercepting and validating tool calls deterministically before execution, ensuring compliance with policies, preventing unauthorized actions, and safeguarding sensitive data. It automates threat detection, policy enforcement, credential protection, budget control, and audit logging, addressing risks such as rogue tool usage, data exfiltration, and regulatory violations. The action provides a robust two-layer defense system, leveraging both deterministic rule-based filtering and AI-driven semantic analysis to mitigate threats and ensure safe AI agent operation.
