## Action Summary

The **AgentMeter Action** tracks and reports the costs associated with running AI agents, such as token usage, model type, and execution duration. It automates the process of recording these metrics, submitting data to the AgentMeter dashboard, and posting a cost summary directly on GitHub pull requests or issues. This action helps teams monitor and manage AI agent expenses, providing clear insights into operational costs without disrupting workflows.
