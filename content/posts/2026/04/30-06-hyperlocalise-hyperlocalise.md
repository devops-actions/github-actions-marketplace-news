## Action Summary

The `Hyperlocalise` GitHub Action automates localization workflows by integrating AI-driven tools directly into your CI/CD pipeline. It provides capabilities such as detecting and reporting localization changes (`drift` mode) and validating translation integrity with pull request-specific annotations (`check` mode). This action streamlines translation management, ensuring consistency and quality while reducing manual effort.
