## Action Summary

Agents Shipgate is a GitHub Action and CLI tool that automates the generation of release-readiness reports for AI agent tool surfaces. It evaluates manifests and tool sources to ensure explicit approval policies, scope coverage, idempotency, and review readiness, addressing the critical question of whether tools are prepared for release. The action provides structured outputs (Markdown, JSON, SARIF) to surface potential risks and blockers before promotion, improving reliability and compliance in AI-driven workflows.
