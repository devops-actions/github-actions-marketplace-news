## Action Summary

The HOL AI Plugin Scanner GitHub Action is designed to analyze AI plugin repositories across ecosystems like Codex, Claude, Gemini, and OpenCode for security, runtime readiness, publishability, and trust signals. It automates the generation of structured reports, including SARIF and submission metadata, while offering configurable modes for scanning, linting, verification, and submission. This action simplifies the validation and preparation of AI plugins for marketplaces, ensuring compliance and quality assurance.
