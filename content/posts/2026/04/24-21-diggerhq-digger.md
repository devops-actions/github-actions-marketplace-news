## Action Summary

This GitHub Action, formerly known as Digger and now rebranded as OpenTaco, provides native Terraform and OpenTofu automation within existing CI/CD systems. It enables secure, cost-effective infrastructure management by running Terraform directly within your CI environment, avoiding the need for separate servers or third-party services. Key features include PR-level locks for safe collaboration, RBAC via Open Policy Agent, drift detection, and support for advanced Terraform workflows like Terragrunt, multiple versions, and static analysis.
