## Action Summary

The **Hosho Prompt Reviewer** GitHub Action evaluates AI agent prompts against prompt engineering best practices to ensure they are effective and optimized. It automates the identification of weak areas across six key quality factors (e.g., scope, structure, context) and provides actionable feedback with specific fixes and improved snippets. This tool streamlines prompt optimization by integrating directly into pull requests, offering automated reviews, scoring, and improvement suggestions for enhanced prompt quality.
