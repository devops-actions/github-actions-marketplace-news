## Action Summary

The Depot CI Snapshot Action captures filesystem changes during a CI workflow and pushes these snapshots to the Depot registry. It automates the process of tracking and storing filesystem state changes, simplifying versioning and deployment workflows. Key capabilities include managing overlay filesystems and supporting seamless integration with the Depot registry for efficient snapshot storage.
