## Action Summary

The **Update Roblox Asset** GitHub Action automates the process of updating assets on Roblox using the Roblox Open Cloud API. It streamlines asset management by uploading new `.rbxm` or `.rbxmx` files, optionally updating their display name and description, and polling the operation status to confirm the update's success. This action is ideal for integrating Roblox asset updates into CI/CD workflows.
