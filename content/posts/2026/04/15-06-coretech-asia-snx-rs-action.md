## Action Summary

The `setup-snx-rs-vpn-action` GitHub Action automates the installation and configuration of the `snx-rs` tool to establish a secure connection to a Check Point VPN tunnel during CI/CD workflows. It streamlines VPN access by handling connection setup, managing authentication, and automatically disconnecting from the tunnel when the job completes. This action is designed for Linux runners and simplifies secure access to protected resources in environments requiring VPN connectivity.
