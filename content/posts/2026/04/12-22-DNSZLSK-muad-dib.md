## Action Summary

MUAD'DIB is a security scanner designed to detect and analyze threats in npm and PyPI packages, addressing the growing risk of supply chain attacks. It automates the detection of known malicious packages and suspicious behaviors using 14 parallel scanners, a deobfuscation engine, inter-module dataflow analysis, and machine learning classifiers. Key features include pre-installation scanning, risk scoring, advanced reporting, and customizable alerting, making it a proactive tool for safeguarding software dependencies.
