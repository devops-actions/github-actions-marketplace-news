## Action Summary

The **GitHub Dependency Submission Action for Mix** automates the extraction and submission of dependencies from Elixir projects using `mix` to GitHub's Dependency Submission API. This enables projects to leverage GitHub's advanced dependency graph, security features, and tools like Dependabot for vulnerability alerts, automated updates, and dependency reviews. It helps improve security, visibility, and compliance by providing insights into both direct and transitive dependencies.
