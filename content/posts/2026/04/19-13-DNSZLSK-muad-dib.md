## Action Summary

MUAD'DIB is a security-focused GitHub Action designed to detect and analyze supply chain threats in npm and PyPI packages. It automates the scanning process using 14 parallel scanners, advanced deobfuscation, inter-module dataflow analysis, and machine learning to identify known threats, suspicious behaviors, and over 225,000 indicators of compromise (IOCs). The tool provides risk scoring, detailed analysis, and safeguards like blocking malicious packages before installation, making it a robust first line of defense for securing software dependencies.
