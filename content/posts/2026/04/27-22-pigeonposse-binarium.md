## Action Summary

Binarium is a versatile and zero-configuration tool designed to create cross-platform executables for Node.js, Deno, and Bun projects. It simplifies the process of packaging projects into standalone executables, making it ideal for streamlining deployments and enhancing compatibility across various environments. Its key features include a fast and user-friendly setup, advanced customization options, and integration capabilities as a JavaScript library, CLI tool, and GitHub Action for CI/CD pipelines.
