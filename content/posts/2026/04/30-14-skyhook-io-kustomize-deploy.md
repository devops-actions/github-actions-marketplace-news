## Action Summary

This GitHub Action, **Kustomize Deploy**, automates Kubernetes deployments by supporting both GitOps workflows (e.g., ArgoCD) and direct `kubectl` apply methods with auto-detection of the appropriate deployment mode. It simplifies deployment tasks by updating Kustomize manifests with image tags, labels, and environment variables, managing namespaces, and monitoring rollout status. The action is designed to streamline deployment processes for both single and multi-image applications in diverse environments.
