## Action Summary

This GitHub Action integrates GitLeaks into your CI/CD pipeline to automatically detect and prevent the unintentional inclusion of sensitive information, such as secrets and credentials, in your codebase. It supports multiple operating systems, offers caching for faster execution, and allows for custom configurations, including mechanisms to ignore specific findings. By automating secret scanning, it enhances security and reduces the risk of exposing sensitive data.
