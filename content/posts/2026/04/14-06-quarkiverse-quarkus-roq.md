## Action Summary

Quarkus Roq is an open-source static site generator designed to simplify the creation and deployment of websites and blogs. It automates tasks such as generating static files from templates and Markdown/Asciidoc pages, leveraging Quarkus for performance and flexibility. The associated GitHub Action streamlines deployment workflows, enabling seamless integration into CI/CD pipelines.
