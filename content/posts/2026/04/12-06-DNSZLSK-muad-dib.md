## Action Summary

MUAD'DIB is a security-focused GitHub Action and CLI tool designed to detect and prevent supply chain attacks in npm and PyPI dependencies. It automates the scanning of dependencies for known threats, malicious behaviors, and suspicious patterns using advanced techniques such as deobfuscation, inter-module dataflow analysis, machine learning, and sandboxing. By providing detailed risk scoring, actionable insights, and pre-installation scanning, it helps developers proactively secure their projects and mitigate risks in their software supply chain.
