## Action Summary

The **Health Reports - Dev Herald GitHub Action** automates the generation of **weekly health reports** by converting CI signals (e.g., unused code, dependency risks, bundle size changes) into structured, actionable insights. It eliminates the need for manual scripting or dashboard setup, providing an efficient way to track codebase health over time, monitor vulnerabilities, and analyze trends. This action supports ingesting data from various tools, enabling teams to maintain a comprehensive history of their codebase's health directly from existing workflows.
