## Action Summary

Agent-BOM is a security scanning tool designed to analyze vulnerabilities (CVEs) across the AI supply chain and infrastructure, including agents, MCP servers, packages, containers, cloud services, GPUs, and runtime environments. It automates the identification of security risks, providing detailed blast-radius analysis to trace the impact of vulnerabilities from packages to exposed credentials and tools. By offering targeted remediation guidance, it simplifies the process of securing complex AI stacks and infrastructure.
