## Action Summary

The **setup-snx-rs-vpn-action** GitHub Action automates the process of installing and using `snx-rs` to connect to a Check Point VPN tunnel during CI/CD workflows. It establishes a secure VPN connection, enabling access to protected resources, and automatically disconnects once the job is complete. This action simplifies VPN management in pipelines, ensuring temporary and controlled access to internal systems.
