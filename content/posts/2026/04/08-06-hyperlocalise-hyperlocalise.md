## Action Summary

The **Hyperlocalise GitHub Action** automates localization workflows within CI/CD pipelines by integrating Hyperlocalise's AI-driven infrastructure. It enables tasks such as detecting planned localization changes (drift) and checking translation integrity, providing actionable reports and artifact uploads. This streamlines localization management for modern applications, ensuring consistency and efficiency directly within engineering systems.
