## Action Summary

MUAD'DIB is a security-focused GitHub Action and CLI tool designed to detect and analyze threats in npm and PyPI packages by leveraging multiple parallel scanners, a deobfuscation engine, behavioral analysis, and machine learning classifiers. It helps automate the identification of known vulnerabilities, suspicious patterns, and malicious packages, preventing supply chain attacks during development or CI/CD workflows. Key features include risk scoring, interactive scanning, safe package installation, detailed threat explanations, and exportable reports.
