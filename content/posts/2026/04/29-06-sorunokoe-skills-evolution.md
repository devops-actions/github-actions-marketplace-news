## Action Summary

The **skills-evolution** GitHub Action automates the maintenance and evolution of AI skill files by identifying outdated dependencies, applying AI-driven updates, and validating file structure. It streamlines workflows by automating monthly health checks, generating pull requests with updates, and providing AI-powered reviews for skill-related PRs. This action helps teams keep their AI skill files accurate, up-to-date, and consistent without requiring manual intervention.
