## Action Summary

The **OpenSCAD-renderer GitHub Action** automates the high-resolution rendering of OpenSCAD scripts into image files, such as PNGs. It simplifies the process of generating 3D model visualizations by handling camera positioning, resolution settings, and parameterized rendering configurations. This action is particularly useful for automating the creation of visual assets in CI/CD workflows, ensuring consistent and customizable outputs for OpenSCAD projects.
