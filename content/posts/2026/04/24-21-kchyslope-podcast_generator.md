## Action Summary

The `podcast_generator` GitHub Action automates the process of generating podcast RSS feeds from a collection of audio files. It simplifies the creation and management of podcast feeds by handling formatting, metadata generation, and compliance with RSS standards. This action is ideal for streamlining podcast publishing workflows and reducing manual effort.
