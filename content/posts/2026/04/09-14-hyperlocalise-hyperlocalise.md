## Action Summary

The **Hyperlocalise GitHub Action** automates localization workflows for modern apps by integrating checks and updates into CI pipelines. It provides two primary capabilities: identifying planned localization changes with a dry-run (`drift`) and verifying localization integrity (`check`), reporting findings as annotations or artifacts. This helps streamline and maintain efficient, AI-driven translation processes directly within engineering systems.
