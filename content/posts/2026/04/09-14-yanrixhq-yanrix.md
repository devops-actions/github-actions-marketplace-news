## Action Summary

Yanrix is an AI-driven GitHub Action that automates continuous STRIDE threat modeling for pull requests by analyzing code diffs and generating a detailed threat model. It identifies architectural risks, evaluates trust boundaries, and tracks changes to attack surfaces over time, enabling teams to integrate security considerations seamlessly into their development workflow. By providing structured findings, risk assessments, and a living threat model, Yanrix helps developers proactively address potential vulnerabilities as their codebase evolves.
