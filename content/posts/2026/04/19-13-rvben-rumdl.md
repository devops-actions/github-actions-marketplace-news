## Action Summary

**rumdl** is a high-performance Markdown linter and formatter built in Rust, designed to enforce consistency and best practices in Markdown files. It automates tasks such as detecting and fixing common Markdown issues, formatting files, and supporting multiple Markdown flavors (e.g., GFM, MDX). With its speed, zero dependencies, and CI/CD-friendly features, rumdl streamlines Markdown linting and formatting for developers and teams.
