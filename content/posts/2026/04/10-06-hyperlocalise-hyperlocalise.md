## Action Summary

The **Hyperlocalise GitHub Action** automates localization workflows by integrating AI-native tools directly into CI pipelines, facilitating the management of translations within engineering systems. Its key features include detecting localization changes (`drift` mode) and validating translation quality and integrity (`check` mode), providing actionable reports and artifact uploads. This simplifies localization processes, ensuring consistency and efficiency in multilingual application development.
