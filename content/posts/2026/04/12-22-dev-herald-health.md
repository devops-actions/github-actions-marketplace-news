## Action Summary

The **Health Reports - Dev Herald GitHub Action** automates the generation of weekly health reports for your codebase by transforming CI workflow data (e.g., unused code, dependencies, and bundle size changes) into actionable insights. It eliminates the need for custom scripts or dashboards by leveraging existing CI signals to track codebase health trends, monitor dependency risks, and provide historical insights. This action simplifies maintaining code quality and security by integrating seamlessly with your CI pipelines.
