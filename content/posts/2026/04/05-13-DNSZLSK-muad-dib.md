## Action Summary

MUAD'DIB is a security-focused GitHub Action designed to detect and analyze supply-chain threats in npm and PyPI dependencies. It automates the identification of known malicious packages and suspicious behavioral patterns using 14 parallel scanners, a deobfuscation engine, inter-module dataflow analysis, and machine learning. By offering pre-installation scans, risk scoring, and detailed threat explanations, it streamlines threat detection and response to enhance the security of software supply chains.
