## Action Summary

The HOL AI Plugin Scanner GitHub Action is designed to automate the scanning of AI plugin repositories across ecosystems like Codex, Claude, Gemini, and OpenCode. It evaluates plugins for security, publishability, runtime readiness, and trust signals, generating structured reports, SARIF files, policy results, and submission metadata. This action simplifies plugin validation by providing both offline and live scanning capabilities, making it ideal for ensuring compliance and readiness for deployment in public or enterprise environments.
