## Action Summary

The **hadolint GitHub Action** automates the process of linting Dockerfiles using **hadolint**, a tool that detects issues and enforces best practices in Dockerfile syntax and structure. This action helps developers ensure their Dockerfiles are optimized, standardized, and free of common errors, saving time and improving reliability in containerized workflows.
