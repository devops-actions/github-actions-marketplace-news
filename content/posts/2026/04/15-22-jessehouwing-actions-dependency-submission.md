## Action Summary

The "GitHub Actions Dependency Submission" action automates the process of scanning a repository's workflows and composite actions to detect dependencies, including nested and forked actions, Docker images, and callable workflows. It submits these dependencies to GitHub's Dependency Graph, enabling enhanced security tracking and advisory integration. This action simplifies dependency management, supports custom paths and patterns, and ensures thorough visibility into dependencies for better security and compliance.
