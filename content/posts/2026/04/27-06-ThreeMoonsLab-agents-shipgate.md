## Action Summary

Agents Shipgate is a static release-readiness scanner designed to analyze and validate AI agent tool surfaces, ensuring that changes to tools used by AI agents meet explicit approval policies, scope coverage, idempotency requirements, and review readiness before promotion. It automates the detection of potential release risks such as undefined approval policies, broad permissions, or inadequate safeguards for critical actions. The tool generates structured reports in Markdown, JSON, or SARIF formats to facilitate human and automated reviews.
