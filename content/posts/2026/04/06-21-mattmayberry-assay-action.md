## Action Summary

The Assay GitHub Action detects and scores the likelihood that GitHub issues, pull requests, and comments are AI-generated by analyzing text for various linguistic and behavioral signals. It automates the process of identifying potential machine-generated content by posting comments and adding labels to flagged submissions, helping teams make informed decisions without manual review. This action is non-disruptive, providing clear score tiers while ensuring no submissions are blocked or automatically closed.
