## Action Summary

Agent-bom is a security scanner designed specifically for AI infrastructure to identify vulnerabilities (CVEs), analyze blast radius, detect credential exposure, and assess risks across MCP agents, containers, Kubernetes clusters, cloud environments, and GPU workloads. It automates the discovery of compromised AI agents, exposed credentials, and potential tools at risk, providing actionable insights on business impact and remediation steps. Key capabilities include multi-cloud and Kubernetes scanning, tool poisoning detection, and enriched reporting for enhanced security management.
