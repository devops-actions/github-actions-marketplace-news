## Action Summary

The **CalVer Tag Action** automates the creation and management of version tags following the Calendar Versioning (CalVer) convention. It streamlines the process of generating new version tags based on dates and ensures they are consistently pushed to the repository, reducing manual effort and maintaining organized versioning.
