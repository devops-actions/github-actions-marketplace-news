## Action Summary

DepVault is a comprehensive web platform that automates dependency analysis and secure secret management. It scans dependencies across multiple programming ecosystems to identify vulnerabilities, outdated packages, and license issues, while providing an AES-256-GCM encrypted vault for safely storing and managing environment variables and secret files. Additionally, it streamlines CI/CD processes with secure token management, secret sharing capabilities, and ensures compliance through role-based access control and audit logs.
