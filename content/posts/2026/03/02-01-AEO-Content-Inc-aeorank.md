## Action Summary

AEORank is a tool that evaluates websites for AI engine visibility by analyzing 23 criteria across discovery, structure, content, and authority. It automates the process of assessing a site's readiness for AI-driven platforms (e.g., ChatGPT, Google AI) by providing a comprehensive audit, scoring system (0-100), and actionable recommendations to improve AI discoverability and content optimization. Key features include a command-line interface, programmatic API, report generation, and integration with CI pipelines to enforce quality thresholds during deployments.
