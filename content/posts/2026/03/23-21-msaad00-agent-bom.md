## Action Summary

The GitHub Action **agent-bom** automates the discovery, scanning, and analysis of vulnerabilities across AI agents, their dependencies, and associated credentials. It identifies the "blast radius" of a CVE by mapping its impact from packages to servers, agents, and exposed tools or secrets. Key features include CVE scanning, secret detection, compliance analysis, runtime protection, and generating comprehensive AI Bill of Materials (BOM) reports for improved security and compliance management.
