## Action Summary

MUAD'DIB is a security scanning tool designed to detect and analyze threats in npm and PyPI software supply chains, leveraging parallel scanners, deobfuscation, dataflow analysis, and behavioral anomaly detection. It automates the identification of known malicious packages (225,000+ IOCs) and suspicious patterns, providing users with actionable insights and risk scores to mitigate vulnerabilities before installation or during development. The tool serves as an educational resource and early defense mechanism to enhance dependency security in software projects.
