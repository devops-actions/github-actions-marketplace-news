## Action Summary

The `eqo` GitHub Action is an accessibility compliance analyzer designed for auditing Next.js projects against all 106 criteria of RGAA v4.1.2 (aligned with WCAG 2.1 A/AA). It automates up to 65% of the criteria, generates detailed accessibility reports in multiple formats (e.g., JSON, HTML, SARIF), integrates with CI/CD pipelines to block regressions, and assists in creating compliance documentation. This action streamlines accessibility testing and ensures adherence to legal accessibility standards.
