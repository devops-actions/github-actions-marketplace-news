## Action Summary

The Aikido Safe Chain GitHub Action enhances CI/CD pipeline security by installing and configuring Aikido Safe Chain to intercept package installations and block known malware in real-time. It supports multiple package managers, such as npm, yarn, pip, and poetry, ensuring automated protection against malicious packages during dependency management. This action streamlines security measures and reduces the risk of supply chain attacks in automated workflows.
