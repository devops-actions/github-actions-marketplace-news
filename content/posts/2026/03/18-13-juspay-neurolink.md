## Action Summary

NeuroLink is a universal AI integration platform that unifies 13 major AI providers and over 100 models under a single, consistent API. It streamlines the process of integrating and managing AI capabilities by enabling seamless switching between providers, offering built-in tools, and automating tasks like memory management, multi-provider failover, and cost optimization.
