## Action Summary

The **load_UWLCM_singularity_image** GitHub Action automates the process of loading a pre-built Singularity container image for the UWLCM (University of Warsaw Lagrangian Cloud Model) into a workflow environment. It simplifies container management, ensuring the required computational environment is readily available for workflows involving UWLCM simulations or related tasks.
