## Action Summary

MUAD'DIB is a security scanning tool designed to detect and analyze threats in npm and PyPI packages. It automates the identification of known malicious packages, suspicious behaviors, and vulnerabilities using 14 parallel scanners, deobfuscation, dataflow analysis, machine learning, and sandboxing. By providing actionable insights and risk scores, it helps developers proactively secure their software supply chain and prevent supply-chain attacks.
