## Action Summary

Hwaro is a lightweight and fast static site generator written in Crystal, designed to simplify the creation of high-performance websites. It automates tasks such as processing Markdown content, managing Jinja2-compatible templates, and generating essential files like sitemaps and RSS feeds, while also providing built-in SEO optimizations. The tool focuses on speed, flexibility, and simplicity, making it ideal for developers managing blogs, documentation, or personal project sites.
