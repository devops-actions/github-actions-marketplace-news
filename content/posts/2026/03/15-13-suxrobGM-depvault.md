## Action Summary

DepVault is a full-stack platform that automates dependency analysis and secure secret management. It scans dependencies across multiple programming ecosystems to detect vulnerabilities, outdated packages, and license conflicts, while also providing an AES-256-GCM encrypted vault for managing environment variables and secret files. The tool streamlines security and compliance workflows, integrates with CI/CD pipelines, and offers features like secret sharing, role-based access control, and audit logs.
