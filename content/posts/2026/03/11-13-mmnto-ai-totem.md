## Action Summary

Totem is a local-first AI control plane and state manager designed to address the problem of AI agents "forgetting" project-specific knowledge, architectural constraints, and prior decisions during development. It automates knowledge retention by embedding a syntax-aware vector index into your project, enabling AI tools to access and enforce context-aware insights before generating or modifying code. Totem enhances multi-agent orchestration, provides defensive mechanisms to prevent AI errors, and operates entirely locally to ensure privacy and Git-native workflows.
