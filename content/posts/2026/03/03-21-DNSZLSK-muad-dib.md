## Action Summary

MUAD'DIB is a security and supply-chain analysis tool that scans npm and PyPI dependencies to detect known threats and suspicious patterns, incorporating static analysis, dynamic analysis, and behavioral anomaly detection. It automates threat identification and response guidance, helping developers assess and mitigate risks before installing packages. By providing features such as risk scoring, interactive scanning, and safe installation, it serves as a free first line of defense, complementing enterprise-grade security tools.
