## Action Summary

The `eu-ai-act-check-action` is a GitHub Action designed to automate conformity checks for AI systems under the EU AI Act. It identifies if the system falls under high-risk categories (Annex III) and verifies compliance with key requirements from Articles 9, 13, 14, and 22, such as risk management, transparency, human oversight, and automated decision rights. This helps developers proactively ensure regulatory compliance in CI pipelines, reducing the risk of violations and costly penalties.
