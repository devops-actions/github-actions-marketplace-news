## Action Summary

The **z64packer-yt-upload** GitHub Action automates the process of uploading preview files generated by Z64 Music Packers to YouTube. It streamlines the task of sharing music previews by eliminating manual upload steps, providing an efficient solution for integrating music content delivery into CI/CD workflows.
