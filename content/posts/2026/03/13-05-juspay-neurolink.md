## Action Summary

NeuroLink is a universal AI integration platform that unifies over 13 major AI providers and 100+ models under a consistent API, enabling seamless integration of AI capabilities into applications. Designed for scalability and enterprise use, NeuroLink supports both CLI and TypeScript SDKs to automate AI stream handling, making AI implementation more accessible and efficient.
