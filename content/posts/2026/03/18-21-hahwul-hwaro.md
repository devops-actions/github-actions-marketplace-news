## Action Summary

Hwaro is a high-performance static site generator designed to transform Markdown content with TOML front matter and Jinja2-compatible templates into optimized static websites. It automates tasks such as content processing, templating, SEO enhancements, and deployment setup while offering features like incremental builds, live reload development servers, and built-in shortcodes for rich media. With support for advanced content management, multilingual sites, and platform-specific deployment configurations, Hwaro streamlines the creation and maintenance of static websites and blogs.
