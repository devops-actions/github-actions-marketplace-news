## Action Summary

The Haydee Workshop Upload Action automates the process of packaging Haydee game mods into `.pack` files using `packer.exe` and uploading them to the Steam Workshop. It streamlines mod deployment by handling tasks such as dependency management, SteamCMD integration, and manifest generation, reducing manual effort and ensuring consistent publishing workflows. This action is particularly useful for mod developers looking to simplify and expedite the mod publishing process.
