## Action Summary

The `vercel-seo-audit` GitHub Action is a CLI tool designed to identify and explain critical SEO issues that prevent Google from indexing Next.js sites hosted on Vercel. It automates the detection of common misconfigurations, such as redirect loops, missing robots.txt or sitemap files, canonical URL mismatches, and other quirks specific to Next.js and Vercel. By providing clear, actionable insights, it helps developers optimize their sites for better crawlability and search engine rankings.
