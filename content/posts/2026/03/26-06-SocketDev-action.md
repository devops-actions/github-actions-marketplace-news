## Action Summary

The Socket Security GitHub Action integrates [Socket.dev](https://socket.dev) tools into CI workflows to enhance software supply chain security. It automates the installation and usage of Socket Firewall (Free or Enterprise editions) to detect and prevent risks during dependency management tasks like `npm`, `cargo`, or `pip` operations. This action simplifies implementing security measures by providing capabilities such as generating firewall reports and caching binaries for efficiency.
