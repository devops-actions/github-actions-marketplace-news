## Action Summary

AIIR (AI Integrity Receipts) is a GitHub Action that automates the creation of tamper-evident, cryptographic receipts for code commits to track AI-generated contributions. It helps organizations comply with regulations like the EU AI Act and audit standards by providing transparent, verifiable records of AI involvement in software development. This action ensures provenance and integrity for AI-authored code, addressing compliance, transparency, and accountability requirements.
