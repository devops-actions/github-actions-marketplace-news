## Action Summary

The Haydee Workshop Upload Action automates the process of packaging Haydee game mods into `.pack` files using the official `packer.exe` and uploading them directly to the Steam Workshop. It streamlines mod deployment by integrating SteamCMD for publishing, managing dependencies, and generating necessary manifests, eliminating manual steps and reducing complexity for mod developers. This action is particularly useful for developers looking to efficiently distribute and update their mods on the Steam platform.
