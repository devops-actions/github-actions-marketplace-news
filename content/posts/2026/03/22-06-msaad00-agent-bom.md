## Action Summary

**Agent-BOM** is a security scanner designed for AI infrastructure and supply chains. It automates the discovery, analysis, and protection of AI agents, mapping critical vulnerabilities (CVEs) to their blast radius, including affected packages, servers, exposed credentials, and tools. Key capabilities include AI-specific BOM generation, CVE scanning across multiple ecosystems, secret detection, runtime enforcement, and supply chain verification, ensuring robust security for AI systems.
