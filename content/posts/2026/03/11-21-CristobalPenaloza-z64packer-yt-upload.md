## Action Summary

The **z64packer-yt-upload** GitHub Action automates the process of uploading preview files generated by Z64 Music Packers directly to YouTube. It simplifies the workflow by handling the upload process, saving time and effort for users managing and showcasing Z64 Music Pack content on the platform.
