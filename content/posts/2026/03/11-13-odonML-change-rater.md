## Action Summary

The "change-rater" GitHub Action automatically categorizes pull requests by tagging them based on the number of lines modified. This helps teams prioritize and manage review efforts by identifying the complexity of changes (e.g., minimal, moderate, or complex). It streamlines the review process, ensuring efficient resource allocation and time optimization.
