## Action Summary

ZIRAN is a security testing tool designed to identify vulnerabilities in AI agents, focusing on the interactions between tools, memory, and multi-step reasoning rather than isolated components. It automates the discovery of dangerous tool combinations, side effects, and multi-phase attack paths using graph-based analysis and autonomous pentesting agents. This tool provides a comprehensive approach to agent security by enabling multi-agent coordination, knowledge graph tracking, and testing of advanced agent protocols like A2A and MCP.
