## Action Summary

MUAD'DIB is a security-focused GitHub Action and CLI tool designed to detect and respond to npm and PyPI supply-chain threats using advanced scanning techniques, behavioral anomaly detection, and ground truth validation. It automates the identification of malicious packages, suspicious patterns, and known Indicators of Compromise (IOCs) across project dependencies while providing actionable insights and mitigation guidance. By combining multi-layered analysis and pre-installation scans, it helps developers proactively secure their projects against emerging threats.
