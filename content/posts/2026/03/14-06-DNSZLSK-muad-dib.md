## Action Summary

MUAD'DIB is a supply-chain security tool designed to detect and mitigate threats in npm and PyPI dependencies. It combines multiple scanning engines, deobfuscation, dataflow analysis, behavioral anomaly detection, and sandboxing to identify known malicious packages and suspicious activities, providing a risk score and actionable insights. This tool automates dependency scanning, threat detection, and safe installation processes to help developers proactively secure their projects and respond to potential risks effectively.
