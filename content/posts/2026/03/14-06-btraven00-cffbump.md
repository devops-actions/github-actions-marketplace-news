## Action Summary

CFFBump is a GitHub Action designed to automate the process of keeping a `CITATION.cff` file up-to-date by extracting author information from multiple sources, including `AUTHORS.md`, git history, and Crossref (DOI lookups). It streamlines the maintenance of citation files by deduplicating and merging author data, preserving metadata, and automatically incrementing the semantic version. This action is ideal for simplifying citation management and ensuring accurate author attribution in research projects.
