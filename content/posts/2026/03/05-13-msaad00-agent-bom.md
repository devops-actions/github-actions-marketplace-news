## Action Summary

Agent BOM is an open-source security scanner designed for AI agent infrastructure. It automates the discovery of configurations, dependency scanning, blast radius mapping, compliance enforcement, and vulnerability detection across various environments, including Docker, Kubernetes, multi-cloud, and AI models. By providing enhanced insights such as CVE severity analysis, compliance benchmarks, and detection of tool poisoning, it helps organizations secure their AI systems and associated infrastructure effectively.
