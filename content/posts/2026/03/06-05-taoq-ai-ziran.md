## Action Summary

ZIRAN is a security testing tool designed to identify vulnerabilities in AI agents, including those with tools, memory, and multi-step reasoning capabilities. Unlike traditional security tools that focus on testing LLMs or web applications, ZIRAN targets the unique attack surface of AI agents by analyzing tool chains, multi-phase campaigns, adaptive attacks, and multi-agent coordination. It automates comprehensive, autonomous penetration testing with features like knowledge graph tracking, real-time monitoring, and support for multiple protocols and frameworks.
