## Action Summary

MUAD'DIB is a GitHub Action and CLI tool designed to detect and mitigate supply chain security threats in npm and PyPI dependencies. It automates the scanning of packages for known vulnerabilities, behavioral anomalies, and emerging threats using advanced techniques such as deobfuscation, inter-module dataflow analysis, machine learning, and sandboxing. By providing detailed risk scores, actionable insights, and pre-installation threat detection, it helps developers secure their projects against malicious packages and protect their software supply chain.
