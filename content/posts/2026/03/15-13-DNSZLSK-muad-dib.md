## Action Summary

MUAD'DIB is a security scanning tool designed to detect and analyze supply chain threats in npm and PyPI dependencies. It automates the identification of malicious packages, deobfuscates code, and provides behavioral anomaly detection, enabling users to assess risk scores and prevent the installation of compromised dependencies. This tool helps developers proactively respond to emerging threats and enhances project security by integrating advanced detection techniques and actionable insights.
