## Action Summary

MUAD'DIB is a security scanning tool designed to detect and analyze supply chain threats in npm and PyPI dependencies. It combines static and dynamic analysis, deobfuscation, behavioral anomaly detection, and ground truth validation to identify malicious packages, assess risk scores, and guide response actions. By providing a free and educational first line of defense, it complements enterprise-grade security tools for quick threat detection and prevention during package installation or dependency scans.
