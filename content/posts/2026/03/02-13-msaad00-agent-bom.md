## Action Summary

Agent-bom is an AI supply chain security scanner that identifies vulnerabilities (CVEs) in packages and images, maps their impact on AI agents, credentials, and tools, and assesses configuration security for risks like credential exposure and privilege escalation. It automates blast radius mapping, risk scoring, and compliance with frameworks like OWASP and NIST, providing detailed insights into business impact and remediation strategies. This action is designed to enhance security posture by addressing AI-specific risks and vulnerabilities beyond traditional CVE detection.
