## Action Summary

Bunkai is a Software Composition Analysis (SCA) tool tailored for Perl projects that automates dependency analysis by parsing `cpanfile` configurations. It identifies dependencies, highlights missing version specifications, detects outdated libraries, and flags known vulnerabilities with remediation suggestions. By improving dependency management, Bunkai enhances the security, stability, and reproducibility of Perl builds.
