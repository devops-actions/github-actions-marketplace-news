## Action Summary

Nucleus is a security framework designed for AI agents, combining a mathematically verified permission lattice with a Firecracker-based enforcement runtime. It automates the detection and analysis of security vulnerabilities in agent configurations by scanning for issues such as permission misconfigurations, unsafe commands, and supply chain risks, generating actionable insights for secure deployments. With its robust verification process and CI integration, Nucleus helps prevent unsafe configurations and enhances the security posture of AI systems.
