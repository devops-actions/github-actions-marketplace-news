## Action Summary

GitGotchi is a GitHub Action that gamifies your coding activity by adding a virtual pet to your profile README, which evolves and thrives based on your daily contributions such as commits, pull requests, and issues. It automates tracking coding streaks and visualizes your activity in the form of a customizable, interactive SVG pet that encourages consistent contributions. Key features include pet evolution, activity-based rewards, streak multipliers, and aesthetic customization.
