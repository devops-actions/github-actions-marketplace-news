## Action Summary

NeuroLink is a universal AI integration platform that streamlines the connection between AI providers and applications by unifying 13 major AI providers and over 100 models under a single, consistent API. It automates tasks such as provider switching, tool execution, memory management, and cost optimization while enabling flexible deployment through a CLI or TypeScript SDK. With robust enterprise features like multi-provider failover and intelligent routing, NeuroLink simplifies AI integration at scale and supports advanced use cases like edge execution and continuous streaming architectures.
