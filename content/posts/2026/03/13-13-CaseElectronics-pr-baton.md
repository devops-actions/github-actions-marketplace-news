## Action Summary

The "PR Assignee Baton" GitHub Action automates the process of managing pull request (PR) assignments by dynamically updating the assignee field based on the current stage of review. It assigns PRs to reviewers when a review is requested and reassigns them back to the author after a review is submitted. This simplifies collaboration by clearly indicating who needs to act on a PR, reducing confusion and streamlining workflows.
