## Action Summary

Depbadge is a CLI tool designed to automate the generation and maintenance of dependency and status badges in project documentation, ensuring they remain accurate, consistent, and up-to-date. By reading project manifests (e.g., `package.json`, `pyproject.toml`, `Cargo.toml`) and using a customizable configuration file, it dynamically creates Shields.io-based badges for dependencies, releases, coverage, and more, and injects them directly into documentation files like `README.md`. This eliminates the need for manual badge updates, reduces maintenance overhead, and enhances the visual consistency and reliability of project documentation.
