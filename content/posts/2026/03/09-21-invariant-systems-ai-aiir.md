## Action Summary

AIIR (AI Integrity Receipts) is a GitHub Action that provides automated tracking and cryptographic receipts for code contributions involving AI tools. It ensures compliance with transparency and provenance requirements, such as those mandated by the EU AI Act, by generating tamper-evident records that indicate whether AI tools contributed to each commit. This functionality helps address compliance, auditing, and accountability concerns for organizations incorporating AI-assisted code.
