## Action Summary

The `evnx` GitHub Action is designed to automate the security, validation, and management of `.env` files within CI/CD workflows. It helps identify and prevent issues such as exposed secrets, placeholder values, misconfigurations, and drift between `.env` files and their examples, ensuring compliance and reducing deployment risks. Key capabilities include scanning for secrets, validating `.env` files, detecting differences between configurations, performing health checks, and converting `.env` files to other formats like Kubernetes secrets.
