## Action Summary

The `llm-model-deprecation` GitHub Action automates the detection and tracking of deprecated or retired language model (LLM) versions across providers like OpenAI, Anthropic, and others. It helps developers identify outdated models used in their applications, provides replacement suggestions, and integrates seamlessly into CI pipelines to ensure proactive maintenance. Its key capabilities include scanning projects for deprecated models, offering deprecation status, and generating actionable reports for efficient model management.
