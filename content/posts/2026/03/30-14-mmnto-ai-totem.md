## Action Summary

Totem is a codebase governance tool designed to automate the prevention of recurring coding mistakes by transforming institutional knowledge into enforceable rules. It helps development teams eliminate repetitive code review issues, such as improper imports or architectural violations, by observing mistakes, learning from them, and enforcing constraints that prevent them from happening again. Its key capabilities include deterministic linting, rule generation, and integration into CI/CD workflows for self-healing codebases.
