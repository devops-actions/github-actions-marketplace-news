## Action Summary

NeuroLink is a universal AI integration platform that consolidates 13 major AI providers and over 100 models into a single, consistent API, simplifying the process of integrating AI into applications. It automates tasks like multi-provider failover, cost optimization, and tool execution while offering advanced capabilities such as memory persistence, context management, and real-time streaming. Designed for scalability and flexibility, it enables developers to seamlessly switch providers, leverage built-in tools, and deploy AI solutions efficiently for edge-first and enterprise-grade applications.
