## Action Summary

NeuroLink is a universal AI integration platform that unifies 13 major AI providers and over 100 models under a single, consistent API. It enables seamless integration of AI capabilities into applications, allowing users to switch providers easily, optimize costs with intelligent routing, and leverage built-in tools and features like multi-provider failover and Redis memory. By standardizing AI workflows and supporting advanced use cases like streaming architectures, NeuroLink simplifies the development and deployment of AI-powered solutions at scale.
