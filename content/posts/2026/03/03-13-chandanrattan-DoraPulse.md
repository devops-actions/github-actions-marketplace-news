## Action Summary

The DoraPulse GitHub Action automates the calculation of DORA metrics (Deployment Frequency, Lead Time for Changes, Mean Time to Recovery, and Change Failure Rate) for repositories or repository groups based on pull request activity. It generates CSV artifacts summarizing these metrics, enabling teams to assess and improve their DevOps performance and efficiency. This action simplifies tracking key metrics by automating data extraction and analysis, saving time and ensuring consistency in reporting.
