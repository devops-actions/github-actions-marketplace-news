## Action Summary

The "SOC2 Compliance Audit" GitHub Action is an AI-powered tool that automates the auditing of repositories for SOC2 compliance by evaluating them against the Trust Service Criteria (security, availability, processing integrity, confidentiality, and privacy). It identifies risks such as hardcoded secrets, dependency vulnerabilities, and compliance issues, producing detailed findings and risk assessments. This action streamlines compliance processes by generating reports, creating issues for critical findings, and optionally suggesting or implementing fixes via pull requests.
