## Action Summary

Agent-BOM is a security scanner and runtime enforcement proxy designed for AI infrastructure. It automates the detection of vulnerabilities (CVEs), credential leaks, and potential attack vectors, while mapping the blast radius of threats and enforcing real-time security policies within the data path. By combining scanning and policy enforcement, it helps secure AI systems against behavioral attacks and supply chain risks, making it a comprehensive tool for safeguarding critical AI environments.
