## Action Summary

Agent-BOM is an open-source security scanner designed for AI agent infrastructure. It automates the discovery of configurations, scanning of dependencies, vulnerability mapping, blast radius assessment, and compliance enforcement across diverse environments including Docker images, Kubernetes clusters, and multi-cloud setups. Its key capabilities include detecting compromised AI agents, identifying tool poisoning, and providing enriched insights through compliance benchmarks and vulnerability databases.
