## Action Summary

NeuroLink is an enterprise AI integration platform that unifies access to 13 major AI providers and over 100 models through a single, consistent API. It enables teams to seamlessly integrate and manage AI features in production environments with tools for multi-provider failover, cost optimization, and persistent memory. Offered as a TypeScript SDK and CLI, it simplifies AI development, enhances scalability, and ensures flexibility by allowing provider switching and advanced deployment capabilities.
