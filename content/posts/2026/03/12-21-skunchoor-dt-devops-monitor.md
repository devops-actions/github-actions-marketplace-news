## Action Summary

The **Dynatrace DevOps Monitor** GitHub Action enables users to seamlessly send metrics and events from their GitHub Actions workflows to Dynatrace for enhanced observability and monitoring. It automates the collection and reporting of pipeline telemetry, including repository, branch, and stage-specific metrics or events, to provide a unified and efficient way to monitor DevOps processes. Key capabilities include zero-dependency implementation, support for custom metrics, and the ability to trigger events even for successful deployments.
