## Action Summary

The **Drawio Export Action** is a GitHub Action designed to automate the export of draw.io diagrams into various formats such as PNG, PDF, SVG, and more. It simplifies the process of converting and synchronizing draw.io files across repositories, enabling seamless integration into workflows. Key capabilities include customizable export options (e.g., transparency, scaling, or embedding) and support for exporting only changed files for efficiency.
