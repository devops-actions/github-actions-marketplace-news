## Action Summary

The **Octo-say GitHub Action** is a simple utility that prints a fun "hello world" message in the form of an ASCII art Octocat greeting the user by name in the console. It automates the task of creating a personalized and visually engaging console output, making it useful for introductory workflows, demonstrations, or adding playful messages to CI/CD pipelines.
