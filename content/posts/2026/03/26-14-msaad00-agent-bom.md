## Action Summary

**agent-bom** is a GitHub Action and CLI tool designed to analyze AI agents, their dependencies, and associated infrastructure for vulnerabilities, supply chain risks, and credential exposures. It automates the identification of blast radius impacts, mapping CVEs to packages, AI tools, credentials, and runtime environments. This action enables security-focused monitoring, posture assessment, and pre-installation checks for AI agents, container images, IaC, and cloud infrastructure, providing comprehensive supply chain and runtime protection.
