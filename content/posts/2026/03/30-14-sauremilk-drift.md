## Action Summary

Drift is a GitHub Action and CLI tool designed to detect and analyze architecture erosion in AI-assisted Python codebases by identifying issues such as pattern fragmentation, boundary violations, and structural hotspots. It provides deterministic, high-precision insights without relying on large language models (LLMs) and automates structural feedback to ensure cross-module coherence and maintainability. This tool helps teams proactively address hidden architectural issues introduced during AI-driven development workflows.
