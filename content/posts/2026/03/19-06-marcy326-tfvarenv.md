`tfvarenv` is a CLI tool designed to simplify the management of Terraform environments and variable files (`tfvars`) by providing S3-backed version control, deployment tracking, and environment-specific workflows. It automates tasks such as managing multiple environments, versioning and backing up `tfvars` files, and streamlining Terraform plan and apply operations with approval mechanisms and deployment history tracking. This tool addresses challenges in maintaining consistent, secure, and efficient workflows for Terraform-based infrastructure management.
