## Action Summary

The **release-gems GitHub Action** automates the process of building, attesting provenance, and publishing Ruby gems to RubyGems.org or other registries, simplifying the release workflow for developers. It eliminates manual steps by handling gem compilation, provenance verification, and registry upload, ensuring secure and consistent gem releases. Key capabilities include support for single or multiple gems in a repository, trusted publisher integration, and optional custom configurations for advanced setups.
