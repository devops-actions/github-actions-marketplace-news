## Action Summary

Feishu Notification is a GitHub Action that sends interactive card notifications to [Feishu (Lark)] channels when pull request events occur. It automates the process of notifying team members about new issues, merge requests, and commits. The action supports different colors for different types of events and handles PRs merged into `main`, with others being skipped silently.
