## What ships

- deterministic SQLite metric computation with a receipt for every figure
- fail-closed numeric grounding before and after suppression
- CMS-modeled small-cell and complementary suppression controls
- mandatory human approval before export
- funder-readable trace view, machine-readable receipts, sealed bundles, and a hash-chained ledger
- fail-closed metric-mapping review queue for anonymized schemas
- English and Spanish report output
- optional policy-gated Bedrock prose drafting, off by default
- reusable GitHub Action for receipt-drift verification
