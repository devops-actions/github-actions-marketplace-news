## Action Summary

Jego is a lightweight static site builder that uses YAML files to generate websites with full HTML and CSS support. It automates the process of creating and rendering static web pages, enabling fast and safe single-core builds while supporting custom themes. Designed for simplicity and efficiency, it eliminates the need for complex dependencies, making it ideal for small to medium-sized projects across multiple platforms.
