## Action Summary

The MinIO Upload Action is a cross-platform GitHub Action designed to automate the upload of files and directories to MinIO object storage. It simplifies artifact storage by supporting multiple sources, glob patterns, automatic bucket creation, and custom target paths, while providing detailed outputs of uploaded files and counts. This action is ideal for streamlining workflows that require efficient and secure transfer of build artifacts or other data to MinIO.
