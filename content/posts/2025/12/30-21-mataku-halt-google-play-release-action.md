## Action Summary

The "Halt Google Play Release Action" is a GitHub Action designed to programmatically stop a release in the Google Play Console for a specified track and version. It automates the process of halting problematic or emergency releases, providing support for various authentication methods and detailed logging for troubleshooting. This action is particularly useful for managing release rollbacks or preventing the distribution of flawed updates to users.
