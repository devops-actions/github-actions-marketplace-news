## Release notes

This release introduces the first stable version of the Bluejay Simulation Action, allowing teams to seamlessly integrate AI-agent testing into their CI/CD pipelines.

Features included in v1.0.0:

Queue Bluejay simulation runs from any workflow

Optionally wait for simulation completion

Enforce minimum quality thresholds

Support for prompts, knowledge bases, and Digital Human IDs

Works for voice, chat, and multimodal agent evaluations

Use this action to automatically validate your agent behavior, detect regressions earlier, and maintain high-quality conversational AI in production.
