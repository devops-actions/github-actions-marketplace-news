## Action Summary

The **action-publint** GitHub Action automates the process of linting a `package.json` file for potential packaging errors using Publint. It ensures the validity and quality of package configurations by identifying issues and providing feedback at configurable severity levels. This action simplifies detecting and resolving packaging problems, improving the reliability and compliance of npm packages.
