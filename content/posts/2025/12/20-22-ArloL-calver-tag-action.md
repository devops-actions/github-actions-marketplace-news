## Action Summary

The **CalVer Tag Action** is a GitHub Action designed to automate the creation and management of version tags using a Calendar Versioning (CalVer) format. It simplifies versioning workflows by generating new CalVer-based tags and pushing them to your repository, ensuring consistent and automated version tracking.
