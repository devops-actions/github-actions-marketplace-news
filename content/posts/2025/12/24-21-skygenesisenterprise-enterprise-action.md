## Action Summary

Sky Genesis Enterprise CI is a GitHub Action designed to provide enterprise-grade CI/CD pipeline orchestration with a focus on security, governance, and compliance. It automates tasks such as vulnerability scanning, policy enforcement, and multi-language CI adaptation while generating comprehensive compliance reports. This action helps enterprises standardize their development workflows, maintain security, and ensure auditable CI/CD processes.
