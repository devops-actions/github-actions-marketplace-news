## Action Summary

The `action-crosspost` GitHub Action automates the process of crossposting content from a sitemap or RSS/Atom feed to multiple social media platforms, such as Twitter/X, Mastodon, LinkedIn, and more. It helps streamline social media content sharing by filtering posts based on age or URL patterns, automatically generating post messages using metadata, and providing a dry-run mode for testing. This action simplifies the task of keeping social networks updated with the latest content, ensuring efficient and error-free posting.
