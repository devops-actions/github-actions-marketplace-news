## Action Summary

The Rootly Alert Action is a GitHub Action designed to automate the creation of alerts in Rootly via its REST API. It streamlines incident management by enabling CI/CD workflows to trigger alerts based on workflow events, with support for specifying notification targets, metadata, service associations, and deduplication. This action helps teams efficiently monitor deployments, manage incidents, and ensure timely notifications to relevant stakeholders.
