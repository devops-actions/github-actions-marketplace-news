## Action Summary

ZoomRecovery is a macOS utility designed to automatically resolve Zoom Error 1132, which prevents users from joining or hosting meetings. It automates the recovery process by cleaning up Zoom-related user data and relaunching the application, eliminating the need for manual troubleshooting.
