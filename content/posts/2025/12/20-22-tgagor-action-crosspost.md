## Action Summary

The `action-crosspost` GitHub Action automates the process of sharing content from a sitemap or RSS/Atom feed to multiple social media platforms (e.g., Twitter/X, Mastodon, LinkedIn). It allows users to filter posts by age or URL patterns, customize message templates with metadata, and run in a dry-run mode to preview posts before publishing. This action simplifies and streamlines crossposting, saving time and ensuring consistent sharing of content across networks.
