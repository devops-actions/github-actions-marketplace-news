The `podcast-generator` GitHub Action automates the creation and management of podcast feeds by generating XML files compliant with podcasting standards. It simplifies the process of maintaining podcast metadata, reducing manual effort and ensuring consistent feed updates. This action is ideal for streamlining podcast publishing workflows.
