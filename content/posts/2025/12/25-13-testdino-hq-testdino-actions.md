## Action Summary

The **TestDino Reporter** GitHub Action automates the process of uploading Playwright test results to TestDino, enabling centralized test analytics, reporting, and debugging. It streamlines workflows by supporting the upload of test reports, traces, screenshots, videos, and other artifacts directly from your repository. This action is designed to simplify test result management and enhance visibility into test outcomes for improved debugging and analysis.
